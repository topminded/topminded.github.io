1. Differentaite Jusi Fabric and Banana Fabric.​


Answer:

Jusi Fabric is a fine sheer Philippine fabric for dresses or shirts that is made in abaca fibers. It has a polished texture and a natural off-white color.

While Banana fabric is produced from banana stems and peels. Banana fabric is the organic, strongest, and natural fabric, composed of lignin and cellulose. The process of extraction of fiber involves a very intensive labor method.

2. what is banana fabric​


Answer:

Banana fabric is a beautiful, animal-free textile that mimics real silk, and acts as a great vegan alternative. The fiber material comes from the stalk of the banana plant, and while it is certainly a unique idea, it is not new.
