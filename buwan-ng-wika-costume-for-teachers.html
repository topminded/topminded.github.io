PROBLEM

Myrna joined a dance presentation for the “Buwan ng Wika." She bought a kimona at Php169.75 and a floral skirt at Php185.25 for her costume. If mother gave her Php500.00, how much change did she return to her mother?

====================================

ANSWERS

1. What is the number sentence?

B. Php500 - (Php169.75 + Php185.25) - N

The mother gave her Php500.00, She bought a kimona at Php169.75 and a floral skirt at Php185.25 for her costume. That's why the answer is letter B. Php500 - (Php169.75 + Php185.25) - N.

2. What is the answer of the above problem?

C. Php145.00

Php500 - (Php169.75 + Php185.25) - N

Php500 - Php169.75 + Php185.25 = N

Add Php169.75 + Php185.25 together

= 355

500 - 355 Subtract the numbers

= Php145.00
