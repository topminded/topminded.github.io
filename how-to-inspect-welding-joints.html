5. a weld joint formed when two joint member are positioned at an approximate 90 degrees angle with the weld joint at the outside of the member?​


Answer:

Tee Welding Joint. Tee welding joints are formed when two members intersect at a 90 deg angle which makes the edges come together in the center of a plate or component. Tee Joints are considered a type of fillet weld, and can also be made when a pipe or tube is welded onto a base plate.
