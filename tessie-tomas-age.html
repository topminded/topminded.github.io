27. The sum of the ages of Sam and Thomas is 27 years and seven years ago,Tomas was three years more than one fourth as old as Sam.Find their present ages.


ANSWER:  Sam is 15 years old and Tomas is 12 years old.

Present Ages:

Sam: x              

Thomas: y

x + y = 27

y = 27 – x


Ages seven years ago:

 

Sam:  x – 7

Thomas: 27–x–7  or  20-x

Solution:

20 – x = 1/4 (x-7) + 3

20 – x = x/4 – 7/4 + 3

(4) [20-x = x/4 – 7/4 + 3] (4)

80 – 4x = x – 7 + 12

4x + x = 80 – 5

5x/5 = 75/5

x = 15  (Sam's age)

 

Present ages:

Sam:  15 years old             

Thomas: 27 – 15 = 12 years old

 

Check:

Present Ages:

15 + 12 = 27

27 = 27 (True)


Ages Seven Years Ago:

 

12 – 7 = 1/4(15-7) + 3

5 = 1/4 (8) + 3

5 = 2 + 3

5 = 5  (True)
