13. a player kick 0.40 kg football with a force of 25 N. what is the accelaration of the football?


#BrainlyFast

Problem:

A player kick 0.40 kg football with a force of 25 N. what is the acceleration of the football?

Given Data:

m ( mass ) = 0.4 kg

F ( Force ) = 25 N or 25 kg·m/s²

a ( acceleration ) = ? unknown

Solution:

We can use the formula ( F = m × a ) in order to solve the problem: to find (a), we will its derivation formula  (a = F ÷ m)

a = F ÷ m

  = 25 kg·m/s² ÷ 0.4 kg

  = 62.5 m/s²

Answer:

The acceleration of the football will be 62.5 m/s²

Force and acceleration is directly proportional. Force and mass is also directly proportional
