½ cup Nature's Promise vegetable broth

1 tbsp butter

Steps

Combine the vegetables and broth in a medium saucepan. Cook on medium 5-10 min., until heated through, stirring occasionally.

Drain broth from pan. To pan, add the butter, garlic, and Parmesan. Season with pepper to taste. Mix until well combined. Garnish with the fresh parsley.

21. dish with procedure and recipe.​


Answer:

Egg in a Blanket

Ingredients Of Egg in a Blanket

2 Eggs

2 Brown bread slices, sliced

2 Tbsp Butter

To taste Chilli flakes

To taste Oregano

To taste Salt

Procedure:

Using a heart-shaped cookie cutter, cut out the centre of each of the bread slices.

Heat 1 Tbsp butter in a pan and place one bread slice on it. Keep the flame on low, and let it brown. Then flip it on the other side.

Slowly break an egg into the heart-shaped cavity. Cover the pan with a lid, and let it cook for 3-4 minutes. Check if the egg has cooked as per your liking.

Slide it onto a plate and repeat the process with the other bread slice.

Season with salt, chilli flakes and oregano. Serve hot.
