A trencher is a piece of construction equipment used to dig trenches, especially for laying pipes or electrical cables, for installing drainage, or in preparation for trench warfare.

Trenchers may range in size from walk-behind models, to attachments for a skid loader or tractor, to very heavy tracked heavy equipment.

19. Identify the type of simple machine that can be used in the given situations. Write your answer on a separate sheet of paper.1.opening a can of sardines2.digging soil3.raising a flag4.chipping a wood5.using a stair​


Answer:

1. Opening a can of sardines - A simple machine that can be used here is a lever. Specifically, a type of lever called a wheel and axle, which is used to rotate the can opener and lift the lid.

2. Digging soil - A simple machine that can be used here is a shovel, which is a type of inclined plane. The blade of the shovel is pushed into the soil at an angle, which reduces the force needed to lift the soil.

3. Raising a flag - A simple machine that can be used here is a pulley. The rope or cable attached to the flag is wrapped around a wheel or pulley, allowing the user to apply a smaller force to raise the flag.

4. Chipping a wood - A simple machine that can be used here is a wedge. A wedge can be used to split a piece of wood by exerting force on the narrow end, which in turn exerts force on the wider end and causes the wood to split.

5. Using a stair - A stair is not a simple machine. It is a structure made up of multiple steps that allows a person to move from one level to another. However, stairs can be designed to be easier to climb using simple machines such as inclined planes or stairs with a low slope.
