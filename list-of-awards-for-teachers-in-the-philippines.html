12. What are the various prestigious literary awards and competitions in the Philippines?​


Answer:

The Don Carlos Palanca Memorial Awards for Literature, popularly known as the Palanca Awards, are a set of literary awards for Philippine writers. Usually referred to as the "Pulitzer Prize of the Philippines", it is the country's highest literary honor in terms of prestige.

The Ramon Magsaysay Award is Asia’s premier prize and highest honor—awarded to those who showed transformative leadership in Asia and has given selfless service and unparalleled effort towards human development.

The National Artist of the Philippines is an award given to exemplary Filipinos who have made significant contributions to the development of Philippine art.

The National Scientist of the Philippines is the highest award given to brilliant Filipino scientists by the Philippine government.

Given by the Manunuri ng Pelikulang Pilipino, Gawad Urian Awards acknowledges and celebrates the excellence in the cinematic industry achieved by directors, actors, films, music, documentaries, and more


13. how different is this national honor from the national artist of the Philippines award? compare and contrast the two awards​


Answer:

The National Artist Award is the highest distinction bestowed upon Filipino Artists whose body of work is recognized by their peers and more importantly by their countrymen as sublime expression of Philippine music, dance, theatre, visual arts, literature, film and media, arts, architecture and design while national honor is an honor for the whole country and it's people.
