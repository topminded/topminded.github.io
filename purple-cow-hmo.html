9. A farm has 5 cows and 10 white cows. A fence is open and two cows escape. What is the probability that it will be a brown cow,then a white cow?


Answer:

The probability of selecting a brown cow first is 5/(5+10) = 1/3, since there are 5 brown cows out of 15 total cows.

After one brown cow has escaped, there are 4 brown cows and 10 white cows left. So the probability of selecting a white cow second is 10/(4+10) = 5/7.

Therefore, the probability of selecting a brown cow first and a white cow second is:

(1/3) * (5/7) = 5/21, or approximately 0.238.

Answer:

The probability of the first cow being brown is 5/15 (since there are a total of 5 brown cows and 10 white cows, for a total of 15 cows).

After the first cow escapes, there are 4 brown cows and 10 white cows left on the farm. The probability of the second cow being white, given that the first cow was brown, is 10/14 (since there are now 14 cows left on the farm, of which 10 are white).

Therefore, the probability of both events happening in sequence (brown cow, then white cow) is:

(5/15) x (10/14) = 1/3 x 5/7 = 5/21

So the probability that it will be a brown cow, then a white cow, is 5/21.

PhilHealth is the health insurance provider run by the government. It is an affordable and progressive insurance program that extends financial assistance to all citizens seeking medical help, whether employed or unemployed. Membership is compulsory for all employees and half of the monthly contribution is covered by the employer while the other half is deducted from the employees’ salary. The amount of financial assistance it extends to its members will vary according to the disease.

If you want to learn more, check out our extensive coverage about PhilHealth and find out its benefits and advantages for members.

HMOHealth Maintenance Organizations (HMO) are private providers of healthcare insurance, except they give you access to doctors within their network. Plans are usually comprehensive and customizable but are only limited to a certain amount annually. The bigger the premium the employer or individual is paying, the higher the annual allowance.

HMOs administer programs such as:

inpatient/outpatient services

surgeries

other ancillary services such as laboratory tests and medication

Some of the well-known HMO providers in the Philippines are Medicard, Maxicare, Intellicare, and Fortune Care. This is commonly provided to employees in private companies, on top of their PhilHealth membership contribution.
