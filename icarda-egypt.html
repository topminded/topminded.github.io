2. what is the exposition in prince of egypt?what is the rising action in prince of egypt?what is the climax in prince of egypt?what is the falling action in prince of egypt?what is the resolution prince of egypt?​


The Prince of Egypt

"The Prince of Egypt" is about the story of Moses, an Egyptian prince, that is based on the biblical book of Exodus.

Here are the answers to your activity:

Exposition:

The film introduces the main characters, Moses and his adoptive brother Ramses. They have their privileged life as princes of Egypt. It also introduces the audience to the cruelty done by Egyptian leaders during the time.

Rising Action:

When Moses discovers his true identity as a Hebrew and witnesses the suffering of his people. The Pharaoh requested Moses to leave the palace and went to the desert for a new start.

Climax:

It happened when God speaks to Moses through a burning bush. He was told that he will be the one who will lead Hebrews out of the Egyptian leaders' hands. Moses returns to Egypt and informed Ramses, demanding that he free the Hebrew slaves, and if he do not follow, plagues will come.

Falling Action:

The falling action begins as the plagues are delivered by God on Egypt such as the death of the firstborn sons. Also, when the Hebrews find themselves trapped between the Red Sea and the Egyptian army with nowhere to go.

Resolution:

The resolution comes when Moses divides the Red Sea with God's help, allowing them to cross safely to the other side. The Egyptian army is drowned as the waters close in on them. The film ends with the Hebrews singing a song of triumph and Moses receiving the Ten Commandments on Mount Sinai, setting the foundation for a new era of freedom and faith.

4. Napag-isa sa kaniyang paghahari ang Upper Egypt at Lower Egypt


Answer:

Narmer, the king who united upper and lower egypt.

Answer:

Si Narmer, na isang paraon, ang nakapag-isa sa Upper at Lower Egypt sa kaniyang paghahari.

Explanation:

Si Narmer ay isang paraon ng Panahon ng simulaing pangdinastiya ng Ehipto (ca. ika-31 siglo BCE).

Siya ay pinaniniwalaan na kahalili ng mga paraon ng Panahong protodinastiko ng Ehipto na si Haring Alakdan (o Selk) at/o Ka. Siya ay itinuturing na tagapag-isa ng Ehipto at tagapagtatag ng Unang dinastiya ng Ehipto at kaya ang unang paraon ng nagkaisang Ehipto. Ang kaniyang pagkakakilanlan ay paksa ng patuloy na debate bagaman sa nananaig na Ehiptolohiya, siya ay kinikilala bilang si paraon Menes. Siya ay binibigyan rin ng kredito sa pag-iisa ng Sinaunang Ehipto bilang unang paraon. Ito ay batay sa Paletang Narmer na nagpapakita at naglalarawan kay Narmer bilang tagapag-isa ng Ehipto at ang dalawang mga selyo ng nekropolis mula sa Abydos na nagpapakita sa kaniya bilang unang hari ng Unang dinastiya ng Ehipto.

16. noong gitnang kaharian sa kabihasnang egypt,sino ang nagpabagsak sa egypt?​


Answer:

Ang Gitnang Kaharian ng Ehipto ang panahon sa kasaysayan ng sinaunang Ehipto na sumasaklaw mula sa pagkakatatag ng Ikalabingisang Dinastiya ng Ehipto hanggang sa wakas ng Ikalabingtatlong Dinastiya ng Ehipto sa pagitan ng 2055 BCE at 1650 BCE, bagam ang ilang mga manunulat ay nagsasama ng Ikalabingtatlo at Ikalabingapat na mga dinastiya sa Ikalawang Pagitang Panahon ng Ehipto. Sa panahong ito, ang puneraryong kulto ni Osiris ay nanaig sa relihiyon sikat ng Ehipto. Ang panahong ito ay binubuo ng dalawang mga yugto, ang ikalabingisang Dinastiya na pinamunuan mula sa Thebes at ang ikalabingdalawang Dinastiya na nakasentro sa el-Lisht. Ang dalawang mga dinastiyang ito ay orihinal na itinuturing na buong sakop ng nagkakaisang kahariang ito ngunit itinuturing na ngayon ng mga historyan na ang ikalabingtatlong dinastirya na bahaging kabilang sa Gitnang Kaharian ng Ehipto.

22. what is the character in prince of egypt?what is the protagonist in prince of egypt?what is the antagonist in prince of egypt?what is the conflict in prince of egypt?what is man vs. man, man vs. self, man vs. society, man vs. supernatural in prince of egypt?what is the point of view in prince of egypt?what is the first person and third person in prince of egypt?what is the theme in prince of egypt?​


Answer:

1.The main character in Prince of Egypt is Moses.

2.The protagonist in Prince of Egypt is also Moses, as the story follows his journey from a prince of Egypt to a leader of the Hebrews.

3.The antagonist in Prince of Egypt is Pharaoh Ramses II, who refuses to release the Hebrews from slavery.

4.The main conflict in Prince of Egypt is the struggle between the Hebrews and the Egyptians, with Moses caught in the middle.

5.There are elements of man vs. man (Moses vs. Ramses), man vs. self (Moses struggling with his identity and purpose), man vs. society (the Hebrews' struggle for freedom and equality), and man vs. supernatural (the plagues sent by God).

6.The point of view in Prince of Egypt is third person, with occasional glimpses into Moses' thoughts through voiceover narration.

7.The theme of Prince of Egypt is the transformative power of faith and the importance of freedom and equality for all people.

27. why is Egypt divided into lower and upper egypt?​


Answer:

To the north was Lower Egypt, where the Nile stretched out with its several branches to form the Nile Delta. To the south was Upper Egypt, stretching to Aswan. The terminology "Upper" and "Lower" derives from the flow of the Nile from the highlands of East Africa northwards to the Mediterranean Sea.
