1. A ramp in Parking garage rises to connect two levels of Parking space. The two levels are parallel with each other. The ramp makes 15 with the lower horizontal what angle does the ramp make with the upper horizontal?​


Answer:

Since the two levels of parking space are parallel, the ramp forms a right triangle with the lower horizontal and the hypotenuse being the ramp itself. The angle between the ramp and the lower horizontal is 15 degrees.

Let's call the angle between the ramp and the upper horizontal "x". Since the sum of the angles in a triangle is 180 degrees, we know that the third angle in the right triangle formed by the ramp, lower horizontal, and hypotenuse is 90 degrees. Therefore, we can use the fact that the sum of the angles in a triangle is 180 degrees to find x:

x + 90 + 15 = 180

Simplifying this equation gives:

x = 75

Therefore, the angle between the ramp and the upper horizontal is 75 degrees.

25. Kendrick holds the rubber blue ball halfway up the ramp and releases ir (figure 1).The ball rolls down the ramp and along the floor beyond the end of the ramp. What force caused the ball to roll down the ramp?


Answer:

The force that caused the ball to roll down the ramp is gravity. Gravity is the force that pulls objects towards each other, and in this case, it is the force that pulls the ball towards the center of the Earth. As the ball is released from its position halfway up the ramp, gravity causes it to accelerate downward, rolling down the ramp and along the floor beyond the end of the ramp. The steeper the ramp, the greater the force of gravity and the faster the ball will roll down the ramp.
