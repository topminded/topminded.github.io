Further Explanation

One variable linear equation is an equation that has a variable and the exponent number is one.

Can be stated in the form:

[tex]\large {\boxed {\bold {ax = b}}[/tex]

or

ax + b = c, where a, b, and c are constants, x is a variable

Whereas the two-variable linear equation is a linear equation that has 2 variables and the exponent is one

Can be stated in the form:

[tex]\large {\boxed {\bold {ax + bx = c}}}[/tex]

x, y = variable

In the statement of the task:

Kevin is 3 times as old as Daniel.

Let Kevin = K, Daniel = D

then the age comparison :

 K = 3D ⇒ equation 1

4 years ago, Kevin was 5 times as old as Daniel

then the equation becomes

K-4 = 5 (D-4)

K-4 = 5D-20

K = 5D-16 ⇒ equation 2

Both of these equations are equations that have 2 variables, so the solution can be used in 3 ways:

1. graph system

the solution is the intersection point of the two equations / lines.

2. substitution

the solution is we change one variable into another variable form of an equation, then substitutes that variable in another equation

3. elimination

the solution is to eliminate one variable from the equation system.

to determine the variable x we ​​eliminate the variable y first, or vice versa.

We use the second method: substitution

we substitute equation 1 into equation 2:

[tex]\rm 3D=5D-16\\\\2D=16\\\\D=\boxed{\bold{8}}\\\\K=3\times 8=\boxed{\bold{24}}[/tex]

Algebraic expressions in mathematics are a combination of coefficients, numbers, variables, constants, and arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

The main composition of algebraic expressions are:  

1. phrases  

algebraic forms separated by arithmetic operations

13. narrate the life and work of santiago suarez​


Answer:

SANTIAGO SUAREZ

One of the most prolific Filipino musical composers, Santiago S. Suarez was born on May 23, 1901, in Sampaloc, Manila.

Suarez studied at the Ateneo de Manila in Intramuros. He took up music at the University of the Philippines and trained under the noted musicians like Cayetano Jacobe, Pedro Eloriaga and Nicanor Abelardo.

He composed more than 400 pieces of harana, balitaw, danzas and ballads.

Among his compositions:

"La Patrona" is a Mexican telenovela that tells the story of Gabriela Suarez, a young woman who returns to her hometown after living abroad for many years. Gabriela is a strong and independent woman who is determined to clear her father's name, who was accused of a crime he did not commit.

As the story unfolds, Gabriela becomes embroiled in a power struggle between the wealthy and powerful Santibañez family and the workers of the steel mill owned by Alejandro Beltrán, played by Jorge Luis Pila. Throughout the series, Gabriela faces many challenges and obstacles, including threats to her life, as she fights for justice and tries to uncover the truth about her father's case. Along the way, she falls in love with Alejandro, who helps her in her quest for justice.

Gabriela Suarez is portrayed by actress Aracely Arambula, while Veronica Dantes is a separate character played by Christian Bach. Veronica is a wealthy and powerful woman who is also involved in the power struggle between the Santibañez family and the steel mill workers.

28. Kevin is 3 times as old as Daniel. 4 years ago, Kevin was 5 times as old as Daniel.How old is Daniel now?


Answer:

Daniel is 8 years old now.

Step-by-step explanation:

Present ages:

Daniel: x

Kevin: 3x (three times as old as Daniel)

Ages 4 years ago:

Daniel: x - 4

Kevin: 3x - 4

Four years ago, Kevin was 5 times as old a Daniel:

3x-4 = 5(x-4)

Find x, the present age of Daniel:

3x - 4 = 5x - 20

5x - 3x = 20 - 4

2x/2 = 16/2

x = 8  Daniel's present age

Present ages:

Daniel : x = 8 years old

Kevin: 3x = 3(8) = 24 years old

Daniel is 8 years old now.

Check:

Ages 4 years ago:

Daniel: 8-4 = 4 years old

Kevin: 24-4 = 20  years old

Four years ago, Kevin was 5 times as old a Daniel:

20 = 5(4)

20 = 20  (True)
