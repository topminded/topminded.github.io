14. The Ladies belt Division of Twice Manufacturing Company is classified as an investment center. For the month of November, it had the following operating statistics: Sales - P675,000 Cost of Goods Sold - 400,000 Total Operating Expense - 237,500 Total Assets - P625,000 Weighted Average Cost of Capital - 5% Twice average stockholders equity is P300,000. It is subject to an income tax rate of 40% What is the residual income of Twice? 3​


Profit margin = (Sales - Cost of Goods Sold) / Sales
= (675,000 - 400,000) / 675,000
= 0.4

division's return on investment (ROI) by dividing the profit margin by the weighted average cost of capital:

ROI = Profit margin / Weighted Average Cost of Capital
= 0.4 / 0.05
= 8

the division's residual income by subtracting the required return on investment (which is equal to the division's total assets multiplied by the weighted average cost of capital) from the division's profit:

Residual Income = Profit - Required Return on Investment
= 675,000 - (625,000 * 0.05)
= 675,000 - 31,250
= 643,750

So the residual income of Twice Manufacturing Company's Ladies Belt Division for the month of November is P643,750.

15. the economic resources of the business is 180% of it's residual interest and it's residual interest is 125% of it's economic obligations. how much are the total economic resources and residual interest of the business if it's economic obligations is 320,000?​


Answer:

Here is your answer!

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's solve the problem step by step:

Given:

- Residual interest = 125% of economic obligations

- Economic resources = 180% of residual interest

- Economic obligations = Php 320,000

To find:

- Total economic resources and residual interest of the business

First, let's find the residual interest:

Residual interest = 125% of economic obligations

Residual interest = 125% x Php 320,000

Residual interest = Php 400,000

Next, let's find the economic resources:

Economic resources = 180% of residual interest

Economic resources = 180% x Php 400,000

Economic resources = Php 720,000

Therefore, the total economic resources and residual interest of the business are Php 720,000 and Php 400,000, respectively, when its economic obligations is Php 320,000.

X = 1.8Y (the economic resources are 180% of the residual interest)

Y = 1.25(320,000) (the residual interest is 125% of the economic obligations)
