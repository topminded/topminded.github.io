12. 1.kon ihan-ay ang fraction 2/5,1/2,1/8 padako,asa man ang mauna​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

2/5 is equivalent to 0.4 in decimal form
1/2 is equivalent to 0.5 in decimal form

1/8 is equivalent to 0.125 in decimal form

Hence, 1/2 is the largest among the three, while 1/8 is the smallest.
