14. Directions: Find the arca of complete answer on the space provided for. 1. Shaded: Unshaded: 16 cm​


Answer:

The arc length formula is x = r theta, where x is the arc length, r is the radius, and theta is the central angle measured in radians.

1. Find the radius of the circle:

We can use the equation of a circle to find the radius:

(x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 = r^2, where (h, k) is the center of the circle, and (x, y) is a point on the circle.

Here, we have the center (0, 0) and a point on the circumference (-8, 0).

Plugging in the values, we get:

(-8)^2 + 0^2 = r^2

64 + 0 = r^2

64 = r^2

r = +/- 8

Since radius must be positive, we can ignore the negative solution.

Hence, the radius is 8.

1. Find the central angle:

We can use the equation of a circle to find the central angle:

x^2/r^2 + y^2/r^2 = 1, where (x, y) is any point on the circle, and r is the radius.

Here, we have the point (-8, 0) and the center (0, 0).

Plugging in the values, we get:

(-8)^2/8^2 + 0^2/8^2 = 1

64/64 + 0/64 = 1

64/64 = 1

2. Find the arc length:

We can use the formula x = r theta, where x is the arc length, r is the radius, and theta is the central angle measured in radians.

Here, r is 8 and theta is pi/2, which is equal to 1.57.

So, the arc length is x = 8 x 1.57 = 12.56 cm.
