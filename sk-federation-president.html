Issues to be Addressed: Low youth political participation in Cagayan Valley Province, lack of knowledge and understanding among the youth on matters relating to politics and good citizenship, as well as lack of access to political resources and opportunities.

Objectives:

1. To increase the political participation of the youth in Cagayan Valley Province

2. To promote understanding and knowledge among the youth regarding politics and good citizenship.

3. To increase access to political resources and opportunities.

Comprehensive Description of your Programs/Activity: The program will focus on three main areas: education, engagement and empowerment. Lastly, the program will focus on empowering the youth to take part in the political process. This will include providing them with resources and opportunities to develop their political skills and capabilities.

Target Participants: The target participants of this program are the youth of Cagayan Valley Province, ages 14-25.

Partners: The program will be conducted in partnership with local government units, civil society organizations, and other relevant stakeholders.

Budget: The estimated budget for the program is PHP 500,000.

Timeline: The program will be conducted within a period of 6 months, from October 2023 to March 2024.

Evaluation and Monitoring Mechanisms: The program will be evaluated and monitored through surveys, interviews, and focus group discussions with the target participants. The data collected will be used to assess the program’s effectiveness and to identify areas for improvement.
