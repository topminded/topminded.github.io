Babysitting can be stressful at times. Stress is defined as an emotional feeling that one experiences whenever he or she is put under pressure. Stress causes a lot of repercussions and greatly affects not only in the individual, but also his or her relationships with other people as well.

Ways to cope stress

Stress can be experienced by children, especially those undergoing the period of adolescence. Thus, the psychologists and psychiatrists alike have devised ways in order to cope stress. Below are some of the mentioned example:

Avoid overthinkingSleep properlyTake medicinesEat wellPractice exercisingConverse with other people to lessen the burden

Causes

Stress is caused by various things. Examples range from minute or minuscule things to more complex ones. Below are some of the known causes of stress:

OverthinkingSchool related worksJob relatedFamily problemsIllnessRelationships with other peopleUnhealthy diet
