1. Share 2 examples of strength, weakness, and challenge about communication ​


Answer:

Explanation:

Example 1:

Strength: Good listening skills

- Listening actively and attentively to others is a key strength in communication. It allows you to understand the other person's perspective, show empathy, and respond appropriately.

Weakness: Difficulty expressing thoughts clearly

- Some people may struggle with expressing their thoughts and ideas clearly, which can hinder effective communication. They may use vague language, speak too quickly or too softly, or struggle to articulate their thoughts in a coherent manner.

Challenge: Cultural barriers

- Cultural differences can pose a challenge to effective communication. Different cultures may have different communication styles, norms, and values, leading to misunderstandings or even offense if not properly navigated.

Example 2:

Strength: Nonverbal communication skills

- Nonverbal cues such as body language, facial expressions, and tone of voice can convey much information in communication. Reading and using nonverbal cues effectively can enhance communication and strengthen relationships.

Weakness: Ineffective use of technology

- In today's digital age, communication relies on technology such as email, instant messaging, and video conferencing. However, some people may struggle with using these tools effectively, such as not checking emails regularly or unfamiliar with video conferencing software.

Challenge: Language barriers

- Language barriers can significantly challenge effective communication, especially in multicultural or multilingual settings. Even with translation services, language, and cultural context nuances can be lost, making it difficult to convey ideas accurately and fully.
