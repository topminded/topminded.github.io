4. it is a sequence of images with background music that forms moving picture​


Animation

Animation is a moving image that comes from a collection of various objects that are specially arranged so that it moves according to a predetermined path at any time and with background music.

The word animation itself comes from the Latin, animationem [nomina animatio] which means to give soul or to animate. There are various ways of making animation, ranging from frame objects [images] by hand, using a computer to other animation-making tools.

Animation Function:

1. As a means of education

Not just images that move without purpose, the function of animation as an educational medium is very applicable.

The use of animation is able to educate the audience more clearly and is easier to understand if it is made by paying attention to the message to be conveyed. Especially for children who we know love watching cartoons, but it would be nice if it was wrapped with an educational message in it.

2. As a Presentation Suggestion

If the previous function was education, then the next animation function was as a presentation medium.

Presentations with the addition of animated objects can really provide further understanding than just using text that looks stiff and boring.

Besides being able to provide a value of interest in the eyes of the audience so that they can focus on the material being explained.

3. As a Means of Entertainment

The third function of animation but the most famous is as a medium of entertainment. That is the reason why the audience feels relaxation or pleasure when watching the animated video.
