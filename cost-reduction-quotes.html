13. Price of shoes after price reduction by 20% is 16€. How much did the shoes cost before the discount?​


Answer:

20€

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the price has been reduced by 20%, then 80% if the price is equal to 16€> Solve the equation below

Let x be the shoe price,

[tex]x - shoe price[/tex]

[tex]80\% = \frac{80}{100} = 0.8[/tex]

[tex]0.8 \times x = 16 \: \: \: \: \: / \div 0.8[/tex]

[tex]x = \frac{16}{0.8} = 20[/tex]

Therefore, the cost of shoes before the discount is 20€.
