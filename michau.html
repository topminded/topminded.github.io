7. Explanation of Enabling Michanisms


Enabling mechanisms are tools, processes, or systems that facilitate the achievement of a goal or objective. They provide the necessary support and resources to enable individuals or organizations to perform tasks or activities more effectively and efficiently.

In the context of organizational change, for example, enabling mechanisms might include training programs, communication systems, or performance management systems that help employees adapt to new ways of working. In the context of technology adoption, enabling mechanisms might include user manuals, technical support, or online tutorials that help users learn how to use new tools or software.

Overall, enabling mechanisms play a critical role in supporting the successful implementation of new initiatives or changes by providing the necessary resources and support to help individuals or organizations achieve their goals.

30. michanical isolation english​


Answer:

Two species are mechanically isolated from one another when their reproductive organs are too different from one another to allow interbreeding between the two species. Mechanical isolation is a form of prezygotic barrier, as it prevents the two different species from mating in the first place.
