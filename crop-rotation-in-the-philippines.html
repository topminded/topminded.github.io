4. What is the relevance of crop rotation in controlling the pest?​


Answer:

Rotating to non-host crops prevents the buildup of large populations of pathogens. The goal of crop rotation is to reduce the amount of the pest population present in the soil. Some pathogens that cause diseases survive in the soil from year to year in one form or the other, usually as sclerotia, spores, or hyphae.
