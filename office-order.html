13. What happens if the back office system in a customer order page is MISSING?


What happens if the back office system in a customer order page is missing?

The answer is the order cannot be transacted via computerized or online system. Therefore, the sales staff or the person who needs to process the order of the customer must resort to the manual method of ordering just to proceed with the sale.    

An online or computerized system is a type of system which a company could use to operate or transact their business process. Almost all businesses owns a system which is customized and specially designed for them. In some occasion, system are having downtimes or a time where a system is having a malfunction or not functioning at all. The causes of these malfunctions varies like a system error, a server is down, the technical team is performing a maintenance, a user accidentally misuse the system and worst, somebody deleted a certain part of the system. Since system is a step-by-step process, it cannot miss a part. If it does, it cannot function well. Normally, staff and managers must know how to transact or revert to the manual process in any case that the system is down.
