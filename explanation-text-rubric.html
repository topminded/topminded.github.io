2. Found.

Explanation: Coherence refers to how well the ideas in a text fit together logically and are presented in a clear and understandable manner. In the given text, there is a clear flow of information from the introduction of the youngest billionaire, who is identified as Bill Gates, to the description of his early career and education, and finally to the key point of his achievement of becoming the youngest self-made billionaire at the age of 31. The information is presented in a logical and chronological order, making it easy for the reader to follow the narrative. Therefore, the text can be considered coherent.

3. Found

The text presents a comprehensive and well-rounded view of the topic, without leaving out any crucial information. Therefore, the text can be considered complete.

4. Found

Explanation: Emphasis/order refers to how the information in a text is organized to give importance to specific points or ideas. In the given text, the writer places emphasis on the key point that Bill Gates became the youngest self-made billionaire at the age of 31. This information is presented towards the end of the paragraph, which helps to build up to the main idea and give it more emphasis. The other details about Bill Gates' career and education are presented in a chronological order, which helps to establish the context and provide a clear understanding of how he achieved his success.
