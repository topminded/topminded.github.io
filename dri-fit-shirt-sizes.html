24. A garment store owner bought assorted shirts. 45% of them were large, 20% percent of them were medium, and the rest were small size. If there was 420 pieces of small size, how many shirts were there?


Answer:

1200 shirts

Given:

Large = 45%

Medium = 20%

Small = 420 pieces of shirts

x = total number of shirts

Solution:

L + M + S = x

(.45x) + (.20x) + 420 = x

0.65x + 420 = x

420 = x - 0.65x

420 = 0.35x

420/0.35 = x

x = 1200

therefore, there are 1200 assorted shirts bought.

For a wet shirt is put on a clothesline to dry on a sunny day, water molecules gain heat and evaporate.

When a clothe is placed on a line to dry, the idea is to ensure that the water molecules should evaporate.

For the water molecules to evaporate, they must gain more energy that will enable them to transit from liquid to gaseous state.

29. A factory makes T-shirts in 5 colors, 4 design and 3 sizes how many different T-shirts can be made? ​


Answer:

60 different T-shirt

Step-by-step explanation:

Following the Fundamental Principle of Counting. If an event has m possible outcomes, and when it has been done, a second event has n possible outcomes, and when it has been done, a third event has p possible outcome, and so forth, then the total number of outcomes is the product of m, n, P, and so forth.

That is mxnxp...

5 colors x 4 designs x 3 sizes = 60 different T-shirt.
