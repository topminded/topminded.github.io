1. what is cross infection?​


CROSS INFECTION

A cross infection is the transfer of harmful microorganisms, usually bacteria and viruses. The spread of infections can occur between people, pieces of equipment, or within the body. These infections can cause many complications.

2. Explain how spread of infectious diseases and cross infection happen?​


Infectious diseases commonly spread through the direct transfer of bacteria, viruses or other germs from one person to another. A cross infection occurs when harmful microorganisms like bacteria and viruses transfer to a patient by way of another person or medical tools and equipment.
