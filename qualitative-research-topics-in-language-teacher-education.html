15. research topic for abm qualitative


Answer:

Title: Exploring the Role of Organizational Culture in Employee Engagement: A Qualitative Study of Selected Companies in the Philippines

Research Problem: Employee engagement is a critical factor in organizational success, yet many companies struggle to create an engaged workforce. Organizational culture is often cited as a key factor in employee engagement, but the specific aspects of culture that drive engagement are not well understood. This study aims to explore the role of organizational culture in employee engagement in selected companies in the Philippines.

Research Questions:

How do employees perceive the organizational culture in their workplace?What are the factors that contribute to employee engagement in the selected companies?How do organizational culture and employee engagement relate to each other?How can companies improve their organizational culture to enhance employee engagement?

Methodology: This study will use a qualitative research approach, specifically, a case study design. The sample will consist of selected companies in the Philippines, and data will be collected through semi-structured interviews with employees and managers. The data will be analyzed using thematic analysis to identify key themes and patterns related to organizational culture and employee engagement.

Expected Contributions: This study will contribute to the understanding of the role of organizational culture in employee engagement in the Philippine context. The findings of this study can be used by companies to develop strategies to improve employee engagement by enhancing their organizational culture. Additionally, this study can serve as a basis for future research on this topic.
