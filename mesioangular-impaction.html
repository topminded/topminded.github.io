17. Explain how you understand the following: economic impact, social impact, cultural impact, and environmental impact.​


Answer:

economic, social, cultural and environmental impact. (answers below)

Explanation:

economic impact- effect of an event to an economic activity in a neighborhood or a country.

It's usually related to businesses, revenues, business profits, personal wages, and/or jobs.

social impact- the effect of the individuals to a community and/or society.

cultural impact- influence of a culture ( history, tradition, etc.) to the community, event, and/ or society.

environmental impact- effect of the people, business, events to the environment.
