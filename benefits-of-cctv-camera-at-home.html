26. Math Challenge 3.Two electricians install CCTV camera's simultaneously for malls in different places. Electricians A installs a CCTV camera every 30 minutes,and B installs every 1 hour and 20 minutes. If thy start installing camera's at the same time at 9:15 AM, at what time will they install camera's at the same time again? Show your solution:


Start to 9:15:

A=30
B=80

A: 9:15, 9:45, 10:15, 10:45, 11:15, 11:45, 12:15, 12:45, 1:15 (every 30 minutes)
B: 9:15, 10:35, 11:55, 1:15 (every 80 minutes)
Solution:
What is the LCM of 30 and 80?
30, 60, 90, 120, 150, 180, 210, 240
80, 60, 240
So the LCM is 240.
240= 240 minutes.
Add 240 minutes (equivalent to 4 hours) to a and b, so they are gonna install a cctv camera every 4 hours.

The answer is 1:15 PM.

Least common multiple (LCM) throught listing method:


30 ⇒ 30, 60, 90, 120, 150, 180, 210, 240


80 ⇒ 80, 160, 240


LCM: 240 minutes


240 minutes/60 minutes = 4 hours


Electricians A and B will install cameras at the same time again after 4 hours from 09:15 hours.
