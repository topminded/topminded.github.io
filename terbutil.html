2. where does tertilization occur​


Answer:

A pregnancy starts with fertilization, when a woman's egg joins with a man's sperm. Fertilization usually takes place in a fallopian tube that links an ovary to the uterus. If the fertilized egg successfully travels down the fallopian tube and implants in the uterus, an embryo starts growing.

Explanation:

location where fertilization most often occur

fallopian tube

In natural conception, the male sperm fertilizes the female egg inside the woman's body. While many think fertilization occurs in the ovary, it actually takes place in the fallopian tube just outside the ovary.
