3. Give the meaning and importance of communication on the following places. 1. AT HOME 2. WITH THE NEIGHBORHOOD 3. IN SCHOOL 4. IN THE MARKET/MALL 5. IN THE WORK PLACE 6. INSIDE THE CHURCH


THE IMPORTANCE OF COMMUNICATION:

At home: it will produce a harmony of the family.With neighborhood: in order to build good relationship in the community.At school: It can greatly affect the quality of learning outcomes.In market: It is needed for trading transactions that are both seller and buyer are profitable.In the work place: important to increase one's motivation at work.Inside the church: In order all the efforts made by church to grow the will be in vain both quality and quantity.

EXPLANATION:

1. AT HOME:

Communication at home takes the very important role. Because in a family, the harmony of the family is determined by whether or not communication is smooth in the family. Communication in the family has the effect of changing attitudes, opinions, behavior or social changes.

2. WITH NEIGHBORHOOD:

Communication with neighbors is also very important. Talking to neighbors is a must. After all, we must have a good relationship with the neighbors. One of them, by communicating well and knowing the ethics. Good communication can be formed by greeting each other and exchanging stories. However, that does not mean that when talking with neighbors, there is no ethics that must be considered. Good communication by exchanging stories, aims to build a good relationship. So, don't let this hurt your heart and make your neighbors feel hurt.

3. AT SCHOOL:

Communication is very important in the learning process in schools, because effective communication in schools greatly affects the quality of a teacher's service to students and will also affect learning outcomes which are also closely related to the quality of education in schools.

4. IN MARKET:

Marketing communication has a very important role for companies to carry out an image of a particular brand. In addition, marketing communications can develop consumer awareness of the products/services produced by the company. So that consumers know the products/services offered, thereby stimulating sales. Along with the times, now marketing communication is better known as integrated marketing communication, which means a marketing communication planning concept that recognizes the added value of a comprehensive plan that evaluates the strategic roles of various communication disciplines.

5. IN THE WORK PLACE:

In the world place, communication is an important element to increase one's motivation at work. Good communication can influence someone to be more active at work. And poor communication can also have an impact on one's work motivation, and cause conflict within the company.

6. INSIDE THE CHURCH:

Communication is a very vital element and really needed in the growth of the church. Without healthy communication within the church, all the efforts made above to grow the church will be in vain. The early church was able to develop healthy communication among its congregation members. As a result, the early church experienced spectacular growth and development both in quantity and quality.
