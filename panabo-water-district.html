28. Does COVID 19 Pandemic affects the Medicine practitioner in Panabo City?​


Answer:

Yes, the COVID-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on medicine practitioners in Panabo City. The pandemic has led to a decline in the number of patients seeking medical care, as they are fearful of contracting the virus and reducing their exposure to health care facilities. This has led to a decrease in the number of appointments and fewer revenues for medical practitioners in the city. Additionally, medical practitioners have had to adapt their practices to adhere to distancing guidelines and the additional use of protective gear, which has added to their costs and reduced the efficiency of their services.
