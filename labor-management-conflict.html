7. is Busting, breaking and driving the labor unions out are just one of the roles of the personnel or human resource manager?


Answer:

Human Resource Manager

Explanation:

Most HR executives have a second role. It is about protecting the interests of the company and strengthening the relationship between employees. Most HR manager roles in contract negotiation include measuring capabilities that support this second job. They are responsible for representing management in consultation with the organization's subsidiaries to achieve the ultimate goal of a collective bargaining agreement that will satisfy management and employees.

Calling to join a union can cause fear in a small business owner, as the role of unions or human resources (HR) in the workplace is different. In a non-union company, HR deals directly with the employee on all labor issues, from salary to termination. However, once a union is formed and becomes part of the business model, HR loses this ability to connect directly as the union negotiates the terms of employment for current and potential employees or is present during termination.
