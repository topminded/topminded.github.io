18. There are 20 e-scooters and cars parked outside a shopping mall. There are a total of 60 wheels altogether. How many e-scooters are there?


Answer:

Let's assume that each e-scooter has 2 wheels and each car has 4 wheels.

Let E be the number of e-scooters and C be the number of cars. We know that:

E + C = 20 ... (1) (the total number of vehicles)

2E + 4C = 60 ... (2) (the total number of wheels)

To solve this system of equations, we can use substitution. We can solve equation (1) for C in terms of E:

C = 20 - E

Then we can substitute this expression for C into equation (2):

2E + 4(20 - E) = 60

Simplifying and solving for E, we get:

E = 10

Therefore, there are 10 e-scooters parked outside the shopping mall. To find the number of cars, we can substitute E = 10 into equation (1):

C = 20 - E = 20 - 10 = 10

So there are 10 cars parked outside the shopping mall.
