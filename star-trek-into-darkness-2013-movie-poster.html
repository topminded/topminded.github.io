15. reflection about Cloudy with the chance of meatballs 2 (2013) movie.​


Answer:

Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs 2" is a 2013 animated movie that tells the story of a young inventor named Flint Lockwood and his friends who travel to an island filled with food-animals, created by one of Flint's inventions.

One reflection I have about the movie is the theme of friendship and teamwork. Throughout the film, Flint and his friends work together to navigate the challenges they face on the island. Despite their differences and occasional disagreements, they ultimately come together to overcome obstacles and save the day. This is a valuable lesson that can be applied to real-life situations where collaboration and cooperation can lead to success.

Another reflection is the movie's message about the consequences of overconsumption and greed. The idea of food falling from the sky may seem like a dream come true, but as the film progresses, it becomes clear that too much of a good thing can have negative consequences. This is a relevant message in today's society where issues such as food waste and overconsumption are becoming increasingly urgent.

Finally, the movie's colorful and imaginative animation style is also worth noting. The vibrant colors and whimsical designs of the food-animals create a visually appealing and entertaining experience that can be enjoyed by audiences of all ages.

Overall, "Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs 2" is an enjoyable movie that offers valuable lessons about friendship, teamwork, and the consequences of our actions.

What is trekking ? Discuss various types of trekking.

Trekking refers to travel by foot through remote destinations that are in most cases inaccessible and unknown to visitors.Trekking is mainly of four kinds:

1 Easy Trekking: This type of activity if for beginners and basically involves the view of colorful horizons or sight of beautiful nature. 

2 Moderate Trekking: Moderate trekking is more challenging than easy trekking. It is performed on medium to slightly higher altitudes. The individuals doing this type of trekking should be energetic.

3 Strenuous Trekking: This type of trekking requires strenuous physical efforts. It is basically done on high altitude regions and is suitable only for those who have some previous experience of trekking. 

4 Difficult Trekking: Fourth type of trekking is done on steep slopes. Generally professional or experienced trekkers do this type of trekking. This type of trekking requires high level of precision skill and endurance by the trekker.
