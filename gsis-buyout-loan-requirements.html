1. The total amount paid on a GSIS loan is P39,900. If the loan was for 3 years at 11% simple interest, what was the original loan?​


Answer: P30,000

Step-by-step explanation:

Let the original loan be x.

Using the formula for simple interest: I = P * r * t, where I is the interest, P is the principal, r is the interest rate, and t is the time in years.

The interest on the loan is:

I = P * r * t

I = x * 0.11 * 3

I = 0.33x

The total amount paid is the sum of the original loan and the interest:

x + 0.33x = P39,900

Simplifying and solving for x:

1.33x = P39,900

x = P30,000

Therefore, the original loan was P30,000.
