3. Direction: Search the meaning of the following.1. Redress2. Representation3. Consumer education4. Healthy environment5. Redress​


Explanation:

1.Redress, reparation, restitution suggest making amends or giving indemnification for a wrong. Redress may refer either to the act of setting right an unjust situation (as by some power), or to satisfaction sought or gained for a wrong suffered: the redress of grievances.

2.one (as a picture or symbol) that is a sign or portrayal of something else. 2 : the act of doing something on behalf of another or others : the state of doing something on behalf of another or others (as in a legislative body) representation. noun.

General Provisions
