29. Damon makes 20 cups of lemonade by mixing lemon juice and water. The ratio of lemon juice to water is shown in the tape diagram. How much lemon juice did Damon use in the lemonade?


Answer:

4 cups of lemon juice

Step-by-step explanation:

total number of cups in lemonade = 20 cups

the ratio of cups of lemon juice and water in one lemonade = 1 : 4

Let 1x be the cups of lemon juice in one lemonade.

And 4x be the cups of water in one lemonade.

Solution :

[tex]1x + 4x = 20 \\ \\ 5x = 20 \\ \\ \frac{5x}{5} = \frac{20}{5} \\ \\ x = 4[/tex]
