[tex]a = \pi {r}^{2} [/tex]
but since it is semicircle then we'll just get half of the area
r = 12 in
[tex]a = \frac{1}{2} (\pi)( {12})^{2} \\ a = \frac{1}{2} (144\pi) \\ \\ a = 72\pi \\ a = 226.19 [/tex]
cost = total area($50)
cost = 226.19(50)
cost = $11,309.50

Answer:

$11,304

Computation:

Solving for the area of the semicircular window by circular formula and multiplying the result by one-half.

[tex]A=\pi r^2=3.14(12\:in)^2=3.14(144\:in^2)=452.16\:in^2\\\\\frac{1}{2}A=\frac{1}{2}(452.16\:in^2)=226.08\:in^2[/tex]

Solving for the total cost of sunblock coating for the window at $50 per square inch.

[tex]226.08\:\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!in^2*\frac{\$50}{\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!in^2}=\boxed{\$11,304}[/tex]
