2. list all the types of improved freshwater fish pond layout​


Answer:

1. Conventional –consist of one sluice gate and long water supply canal. This supplies the different compartments

. 2. Radiating –has one sluice gate; wide and short supply canal; and secondary gates in the different compartments

3. Progressive –consists of one sluice gate; long supply canal and a secondary gates provided to different compartments of progressively increasing areas.

4. Specialized –has one sluice gate; one or two drainage gates. Two secondary gates compartment; and a large supply and catching canal/pond.

20. explain the difference of fish pen, fish cage, and fish pond?​


Explanation:

The only difference is that a fish pen is a fish enclosure where fish from outside the enclosure cannot enter while a fish corral is constructed with openings to serve as fish trap. Examples of species cultured in fish pens are milkfish (bangus), carp, tilapia and sea bass (apahap).

A fish enclosure made of closely-woven bamboo screens, nylon screens or nets or other materials attached to poles staked at the bottom up to the surface of the lake, river or other shallow bodies of water for the purpose of growing and/or culturing of fish to various sizes in fresh, brackish and marine waters.

Fish cage refers to a stationary or floating fish enclosure made of synthetic net wire/bamboo screen or other materials set in the form of inverted mosquito net (hapa type) with or without cover with all sides either tied to poles staked to the bottom of the water or with anchored floats for aquaculture purposes.

A fish pond or fishpond is a controlled pond, small artificial lake or retention basin that is stocked with fish and is used in aquaculture for fish farming, for recreational fishing, or for ornamental purposes.

29. mr bustamante wants to make a rectangular fish pond in his backyard .The perimeter of the pond is 28ft and the area is 48ft².find the dimensions of fish pond.​


Given:

P = 28 ft

A = 48 ft²

Formula:

P = 2(L+W)

A = LW

Solution:

P = 2(L+W)

28ft = 2(L+W)

W = (28/2) - L

W = 14 - L

Substitute the value of W to the formula of the rectangle's Area:

A = LW

48ft² = (L)(14 - L)

14L - L² - 48 = 0

Using the equation, -b ± √(b² - 4ac) / 2a

L = -14 ± √(14² - 4(-1)(-48) / 2(-1)

L = 6ft

W = 14 - L

W = 14 - 6

W = 8ft

To check:

P = 28ft

P = 2(L+W)

P = 2(8+6)

P = 28✓

A = 48ft²

A = LW

A = 8×6

A = 48✓
