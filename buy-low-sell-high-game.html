5. suppose that you buy a rare stamp for $15, sell it for $20, buy it back for $25 and finally sell it for$30. how much money did you make or lose in buying and selling this stamp?


Answer:

$10


Step-by-step explanation:

Purchase price = $15

Selling Price = $20

Profit = $20 - $15 = $5


Purchase price = $25

Selling Price = $30

Profit = $30 - $25 = $5


Total profit = $5 + $5 = $10


Answer: He made a profit of $10
