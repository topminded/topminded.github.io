5. Solve the problem below using PMDAS/GMDAS rule. In a certain drugstore, the pharmacist daughter of the owner helps her mother account the medicines for fever. She finds specialized holder of tablets. She has recorded 4 groups of 10 layers of 10 tablets on each side of the holders. How many tablets are there?​


Mathematical Operation, PEMDAS/GEMDAS and It's Application in Real Life

In the given problem, it is an application of linear equation and solving word problem about mathematical operation. It is a combination of general mathematics and algebra.

The given problem is "In a certain drugstore, the pharmacist daughter of the owner helps her mother account the medicines for fever. She finds specialized holder of tablets. She has recorded 4 groups of 10 layers of 10 tablets on each side of the holders. How many tablets are there?"

Step by step solution and discussion

In the given problem, apply your skills and knowledge in fraction and mathematical operation.

Step 1: List down all the given of the problem

4 groups of 10 layers of 10 tablets on each side of the holders

Step 2: Identify what is being ask

How many tablets are there?

Step 3: Create mathematical operation

The phrase "4 groups of 10 layers of 10 tablets on each side"

It says that for every 4 groups, it has 10 layers. For every 10 layers, it has 10 tablets. So,

4 × 10 × 10 = N, where N is the number of tablets.

Step 4: Solve for the number of buns left

Since the equation has two operation, apply PEMDAS Or GEMDAS in simplifying the equation, but the equation has only one type of operation (multiplication).

PEMDAS → Parenthesis, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction

GEMDAS → Group, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction)

PEMDAS/GEMDAS means, you must perform first the group equation, followed by exponents, and next is either multiplication or division (it was from left to right), then lastly is addition or subtraction (from left to right).

Solving the equation 4 × 10 × 10 = N, just multiply all the three numbers.

4 × 10 = 40, then 40 × 10 = 400.

Thus, there 400 tablets.

Final Answer

400 tablets
