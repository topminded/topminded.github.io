15. Who was considered as the first master of arnis in the philippines


Answer:

Remy Amador Presas

Explanation:

Remy Amador Presas (December 19, 1936 – August 28, 2001) was the founder of Modern Arnis, a popular Filipino martial art. Born in the Philippines, he moved to the United States in 1974, where he taught his art via seminars and camps. In 1982 he was inducted into the Black Belt Hall of Fame as Instructor of the Year. He published several books and videos on Modern Arnis and is recognised as the "Father of Modern Arnis".

Answer:

A business plan serves the following masters in the following ways:

A. Entrepreneur:

Helps to refine and clarify the entrepreneur's business idea and vision.Helps to identify potential challenges and risks that may arise during business operations.Helps to identify potential opportunities and markets for the business.Provides a roadmap for the entrepreneur to follow in order to achieve their goals and objectives.Helps to attract potential investors or financing.

B. Investors and Cautious Financiers:

Provides a clear understanding of the business, its goals, and how it plans to achieve them.Provides a detailed analysis of the industry, market, competition, and potential risks and challenges.Helps to assess the feasibility and profitability of the business idea.Helps to evaluate the entrepreneur's skills, experience, and ability to manage and grow the business.Helps to determine the amount of funding needed and the potential return on investment.

C. Managers and Staff:

Helps to clarify the organization's goals, objectives, and strategies.Provides a clear understanding of the company's structure, roles, and responsibilities.Helps to create a sense of direction and purpose for the employees.Helps to identify key performance indicators and metrics to measure progress.Helps to communicate the company's vision and values to the employees.
