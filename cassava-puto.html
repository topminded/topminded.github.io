Answer:

The problem above is asking for the peso markup and percentage markup of turon. Markup happens when the price of an item goes up. It is based on the markup rate. The peso markup of turon is 4 pesos. Then, the percentage markup of turon is 40%.

How to Find the Peso Markup

The peso markup refers to the amount being added to the original price. To find the peso markup of turon, you have to subtract the original price from the selling price. Thus, the peso markup is 4 pesos.

14 pesos - 10 pesos = 4 pesos

How to Find the Percentage Markup

The percentage markup refers to the rate or percentage of the peso markup from the original price. To find the percentage markup of turon, divide the peso markup by the oroginal price. Then, multiply it by 100 and add a percent sign. Thus, it is 40%.

4 ÷ 10 = 0.4 × 100 = 40%
