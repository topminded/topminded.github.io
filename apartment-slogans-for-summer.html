16. A copper telephone wire has essentially no sag between poles 50 m apart on a winter day when the temperature is 10 oC. How much longer is the wire on a summer day, when the temperature is 35 oC?


The length of the wire increases by 0.0213 m = 2.13 cm

Further explanation

The expansion of objects is influenced by 3 factors, namely the size of the original object, the type of object and the change in temperature.

There are 3 types of expansion in solids

Linear expansion Area expansion Volume expansion

Linear expansion is the increase in the length of an object due to changes in temperature (changes in area and volume are negligible). Generally occurs in long but thin objects.

Can be formulated :

ΔL= Lo .α .Δ T

L = the final length of the object (m)

L0 = initial length (m)

α = coefficient of linear expansion (/ 0C)

ΔT = change in temperature (C0)  

Given

Lo = 50 m

t₁ = 10 °C

t₂ = 35 °C

Required

L

Solution

A copper telephone wire will experience linear expansion(length) when heated.

coefficient of linear expansion of copper : 1.7 x 10⁻⁵ /°C

Input the value :

[tex]\tt \Delta L=50\times 1.7\times 10^{-5}\times (35-10)\\\\\Delta L=0.0213[/tex]

24. In an apartment complex, there are 5 windows in each apartment, and 9 apartments in each floor. How many windows are there on 8 floors?


Answer:

Algebraic Equations

In an apartment complex with 5 windows in each apartment and 9 apartments in each floor, there should be 360 windows in 8 floors.

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this equation, we need to list down the items given, and what we are looking for. This way, we can solve the problem easily and in a systematic manner.

Given:

5 windows in each apartment 9 apartments in each floor

Find:

Number of windows on 8 floors

Solution:

Total number of windows = 5 X 9 X 8

Total number of windows = 360 windows

Therefore, on the first 8 floors of the apartment complex, there should be a total of 360 windows.
