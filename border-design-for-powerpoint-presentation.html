11. What is Good PowerPoint design?


Answer:

A good PowerPoint design means that the arrangement of the images and the content including the template used is clear, clean, simple (or not too complex to view), accurate, and appropriate to the topic of the presentation.

When creating a presentation, always remember to make sure that your design is appropriate to the topic being discussed. As much as possible, limit the words you are going to use. Avoid long paragraphs as it will strain the viewers' eyes. The lesser the words, the easier the topic be understood and remembered.Make sure to make the design simple by not putting too many images at once. Or by enlarging the images too much (unless it is used as a background image) or by shrinking it too much that the viewers cannot see the image well.Choose a good combination of fonts. As much as possible use at most 2-3 types of fonts.Choose a good color scheme or color combinations for your fonts and background color. You can add gifs and animations to make your presentation modern and lively. These will keep the audience entertained and focused on your presentation throughout the discussion.

In conclusion, a good PowerPoint Presentation relies on a good PPT design or template. So, make sure to format a good one!

26. MS POWERPOINT THEMES AND BACKGROUND STYLES OBJECTIVES 1. What are the design themes? 2. What are the factors to consider in choosing a theme for a presentation? 3. Why are themes easy to use? 4. What are the steps in applying a design theme to selected slides in a presentation?


Answer:

1. Design themes in MS PowerPoint are pre-designed sets of colors, fonts, and effects that can be applied to a presentation. They give a consistent look and feel to a presentation, and can be customized to meet the needs of the presenter.

2. There are several factors to consider when choosing a design theme for a presentation. These include the audience and purpose of the presentation, the type of content being presented, and the personal preferences of the presenter. It is important to choose a theme that is appropriate for the content and that enhances the overall message of the presentation.

3. Themes are easy to use because they are pre-designed and can be applied with just a few clicks. They provide a consistent look and feel to a presentation, which can save time and improve the overall quality of the presentation.

4. To apply a design theme to selected slides in a presentation, follow these steps:

- Click on the Design tab in the ribbon.

- Browse the theme options available in the Themes group.

- Click on the theme you want to apply to the selected slides.

- If you want to customize the theme, click on the drop-down arrow next to the theme and select the desired options from the menu.

- The design theme will be applied to the selected slides in the presentation.
