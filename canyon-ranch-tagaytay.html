2. In sky ranch tagaytay, the ferris wheel ride costs 150 php for adult and 120 php for kids.Velasco family bought 12 tickes and paid 1560 php.How many tickets of each kind did they buy?​


Answer: 4 adult, 8 kid

Step-by-step explanation:

Let [tex]x[/tex] be the number of adult tickets bought by the Velasco family. Since there are 12 tickets, the number of kid tickets bought is [tex]12-x[/tex].

The working equation for this problem will be [tex]150x+120(12-x)=1560[/tex].

We can use this to solve for the values of adult tickets and kid tickets.

[tex]150x + 120(12-x) = 1560\\150x + 1440 - 120x = 1560\\30x = 120\\[/tex]

Adult Tickets: [tex]x = 4[/tex]

Kid Tickets: [tex]12-x=8[/tex]

Thus, there are 4 adult tickets and 8 kid tickets.

This is an example of ungrouped data.

The formula to get the percentile is P = [tex]\frac{k(n+1)}{100}[/tex]

where k is the percentile you are looking for

          n is the number of shirts (observation)

Therefore

[tex]P_{50} = \frac{50(10+1)}{100} = \frac{50(11)}{100} = \frac{550}{100} = 5.5[/tex]

Your 50th Percentile lies at 5.5th observation

What does that mean?

Let's arrange your data  from least to greatest

100, 110, 170, 180, 200, 200, 210, 250, 250, 300

In this case your going to count how much your percentile is base from your observation an that lies between

100, 110, 170, 180, 200 (5.5 is here) 200, 210, 250, 250, 300

So, the 5th observation is 200

            6th observation is 200

Whether or not we meet in the middle of two (2) prices which are Php 200 and Php 200, we can easily say that:

Base from your data, the 5th and 6th observation is 200. Therefore we can conclude that the average (which refers to P50) of the price of the shirts is Php 200 or the Php 200 peso shirts are the most in sales.


13. The prices of a souvenir T-shirt in Tagaytay from 10 souvenir shops located at Sky Ranch are as follows: 250, 100, 180, 110, 200, 250, 170, 200, 210, and 300. What is the value of the 50th percentile and what does it mean?


Answer:

200 is the 50th percentile. This means thar 200 is also considered the median of the given data set.

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

The 50th percentile of the prices of the souvenir T-Shirt in Tagaytay.

What are given?

10 shops with the following prices (arranged in ascending order)

100

110

170

180

200

200

210

250

250

300

How to solve?

The data should be arranged from lowest to greatest. We will then find the 50th percentile and then interpret the result.

Formula:

First:

50% * N = k

Where N is the total number of observations. k will indicate the place (kth value) of the data we will use. Then we proceed to:

(kth + (kth+1)) ÷2 =x

x will the 50% percentile.

Solution:

50%*N=k

0.5(10)= 5

This means that k=5. The 5th place in ascending order is 200 and the 6th (kth+1) place is also 200, we have

(kth + (kth+1)) ÷2 =x

(200+200)÷2=x

400/2=x

x=200

CONCLUSION:

The 50th percentile is 200.

What does this mean?

This means that 200 is also the median of the given data set. This also means that 50% of the data falls behind 200 and the rest 50% falls above 200.

20. how are hills and canyons formed?​


Most canyons were formed by a process of long-time erosion from a plateau or table-land level. The cliffs form because harder rock strata that are resistant to erosion and weathering remain exposed on the valley walls.

Hills are also formed because of erosion, which happens when bits of rock, soil, and sediment get washed away and placed in a pile somewhere else. Hills can be destroyed by erosion, as material is worn away by wind and water.
