23. saang bansang Sinilangan ni Hanae Mori


Saang bansang Sinilangan si Hanae Mori?

Si Hanae Mori (森 英恵 Mori Hanae, ipinanganak noong Enero 8, 1926, sa Yoshika, Shimane) ay isang tagapagdisenyo ng moda sa bansang Hapon. Siya pa lamang ang nag-iisang babaeng Hapones na nakapagtanghal ng kaniyang mga kuleksiyon sa mga entabladong lakaran ng mga modelo sa Paris at New York. Siya rin ang unang babaeng Asyano na natanggap bilang opisyal na mayroong bahay-disenyuhang haute couture ng fédération française de la couture (Pederasyong Pranses ng Mataas na Moda) sa Pransiya. Siya ang itinuturing na pinaka naparangalang disenyador ng moda sa Hapon.[kailangan ng sanggunian] Dahil sa isa siya sa unang mga babae na nagkaroon ng karera sa pagmomoda sa Hapon, malawakan siyang itinuturing bilang isang ikono o larawan ng kababaihang malaya.[kailangan ng sanggunian] Ang kaniyang bahay ng moda, na nagbukas sa Hapon noong 1951, ay lumaki upang maging isang negosyong nagkakahalaga ng $500 milyon nang sumapit ang dekada ng 1990.

26. 1. Which of the following best describe your lungs?hana​


Explanation:

The lungs are divided into different parts which are called lobes. The right lung has three lobes called upper, middle and lower lobes. The left lung only has two lobes, the upper and lower.

Where are the lungs found?

The lungs are found in the chest on the right and left side. At the front they extend from just above the collarbone (clavicle) at the top of the chest to about the sixth rib down. At the back of the chest the lungs finish around the tenth rib. The protective linings which cover the lungs (pleura) continue down to the twelfth rib. From front to back the lungs fill the rib cage but are separated by the heart, which lies in between them.


27. The sum of ages of Mara, Hana and Ara is 60, and Mara is 60% younger than Ara. Eight years ago, the sum of the ages of Mara and Hana divided by the age of Ara gives a quotient of 1 and remainder of 2. How old is Mara, Hana and Ara?​


[tex]\mathbb{TABLE:}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|} \cline{1-3} & Present & Past \\ \cline{1-3}Mara & x & x - 8\\ \cline{1-3}Hana & y & y - 8 \\ \cline{1-3} Ara & z & z - 8 \\ \cline{1-3} \end{tabular}[/tex]

[tex]\mathbb{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

First condition: The sum of ages of Mara, Hana and Ara is 60

[tex]\mathsf{x+y+z=60} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 1}[/tex]

Second condition: Mara is 60% younger than Ara

x = 60%z

[tex]\mathsf{x=0.6z} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 2}[/tex]

Third condition: Eight years ago, the sum of the ages of Mara and Hana divided by the age of Ara give a quotient of 1 and remainder of 2

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{(x-8)+(y-8)}{(z-8)}=1+\dfrac{2}{(z-8)}} \longleftarrow \mathsf{simplify}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{x+y-16}{z-8}-\dfrac{2}{z-8}=1}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{x+y-18}{z-8}=1} \longleftarrow \mathsf{multiply \: both \: sides \: by \: z-8}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{x+y-18=z-8}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{x+y-z=10} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 3}[/tex]

Substitute equation 2 in equation 1

x + y + z = 60

0.6z + y + z = 60

y + 1.6z = 60

[tex]\mathsf{y=60-1.6z} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 4}[/tex]

Substitute equation 4 and equation 2 in equation 3

x + y - z = 10

0.6z + (60 - 1.6z) - z = 10

0.6z - 1.6z - z + 60 = 10

-2z = -50

[tex]\mathsf{z=\dfrac{-50}{-2}}[/tex]

z = 25

Substitute the value of z in equation 2 and equation 4

x = 0.60(25) = 15

y = 60 - 1.6(25) = 20

[tex]\mathbb{ANSWER:}[/tex]

Mara is: [tex]\boxed{\mathsf{15 \: years \: old}}[/tex]

Hana is: [tex]\boxed{\mathsf{20 \: years \: old}}[/tex]

Ara is: [tex]\boxed{\mathsf{25 \: years \: old}}[/tex]
