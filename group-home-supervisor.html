How do you speak to an elder?

- Speak Clearly

Remain calm and talk in a gentle, matter-of-fact way. Speak louder, if necessary, but do not shout. Make sure to enunciate clearly and avoid mumbling and talking too quickly. Focus on one idea at a time, and keep sentences short and simple.

How do you speak to an supervisor ?

- Prepare for the Talk

No matter if the news is good or bad, don't just rush into conversation. If you are upset about a workplace issue, approach the supervisor in a calm and collected – yet mildly serious – manner. Alternatively, coming off as overly excited about something positive might appear unprofessional.
