15. 1. The population of Bay Village is 35,000 today. Every year the population of Bay Villageincreases by 750 people.a. Write a linear function that represents the population of Bay Village x years fromtoday.​


Answer:

f(x) = 35,000 + 750x

Step-by-step explanation:

Current population: 35,000

Increase in population per year in succeeding x years: 750

Number of years from today: x

Population as function of x years:

f(x) = 35,000 + 750x

To use the function, let's say you want to know the population in 4 years; x= 4:

f(3) = 35,000 + 750(4)

f(3) = 35,000 + 3,000

f(3) = 38,000
