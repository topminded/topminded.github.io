2. What isductal carcinoma​


Ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS) is the presence of abnormal cells inside a milk duct in the breast. DCIS is considered the earliest form of breast cancer. DCIS is noninvasive, meaning it hasn't spread out of the milk duct and has a low risk of becoming invasive.

Answer:

DCIS isn't life-threatening, but having DCIS can increase the risk of developing an invasive breast cancer later on. When you have had DCIS, you are at higher risk for the cancer coming back or for developing a new breast cancer than a person who has never had breast cancer before

26. Define and differentiate gross and domestic product and Gross national product​


DEFINITION

GDP

-GDP stands for Gross Domestic Product and measures the monetary value of all imports and services produced inside nations geographic borders during a specific period.

GNP

-GNP stands for Gross National Product and measures the monetary value of output and services produced by all citizens of a country both domestically and abroad. However, it does not include the output of foreign residents.

DIFFERENTIATE

GDP

-The strength of the country’s domestic economy.

GNP

-How the residents are contributing to the country’s economy.
