6. Annie bought one dozen smartphones for 200,000.00 with a discount of 5%.she sold half dozen at a price of 18,000 per unit.However,a new model of smartphone became available in the market,so she sold the remaining half dozen 12,000 each unit.compute the operating profit margin rate​


The general formula for profit is

Profit = Revenue – Expenses

ۦۦۦۦۦۦۦۦۦۦ

Revenue in our cases is:

[tex]18000 × 6 + 12000 × 6 = 180000[/tex]

Expenses is the cost of bought smartphones with discount accounted for:

[tex]200000 × (1 - 5\%) = 200000 × (1 - 0.05) = 200000 × 0.95 = 190000[/tex]

Profit = 180000 – 190000 = –10000

Negative profit is called loss.

Answer: she lost Php10000
