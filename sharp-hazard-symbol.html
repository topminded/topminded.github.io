23. what is sharp symbol ​


In music, sharp, dièse, or diesis means, "higher in pitch". More specifically, in musical notation, sharp means "higher in pitch by one semitone". Sharp is the opposite of flat, which is a lowering of pitch. A sharp symbol, ♯, is used in key signatures or as an accidental.

27. describe the use of the symbols sharp ,flat and natural.​


Answer:

A sharp sign means "the note that is one half step higher than the natural note". A flat sign means "the note that is one half step lower than the natural note". Some of the natural notes are only one half step apart, but most of them are a whole step apart.
