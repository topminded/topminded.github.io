21. ano ang mga sangkap sa paggawa ng banana cake ?​


Answer:

Ingredients

4 eggs

170 grm ( 3/4 cup)sugar

170 grm (3/4 cup) melted butter or vegetable oil (or mixture of butter and vegetable oil)

300 grm bananas (weighed without skin) or 2-3 super big bananas

250 grm (2 cups) flour

1 tsp baking powder

1 tsp baking soda

1/8 tsp salt

Preparation

Step 1

Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a big bowl, set aside. Melt the butter in a pot and set aside to cool. Mash the bananas with a fork in a bowl and set aside.

Using cake-mixer, beat eggs and sugar until thick and pale yellow.

Add in melted butter/oil, mix well using a spatula.

Add in mashed bananas, mix well using a spatula.

Finally fold in sifted flour mixture using a spatula.

Prepare the steamer 10 minutes in advance and make sure the water is boiling before you put in the cake. Lined the base of the cake pan with baking paper and greased both the base and the sides with baking spray/oil.

Pour the cake butter into a lined and greased 8-inch round cake pan and steam at high heat for 45-50 minutes

27. Dimple is making 2 ½ batches of banana cake to her school’s annual bake sale. If one batch of banana cake requires 1 ¼ cups of flour, how many cups of flour does Dimple need to make 2 ½ batches of banana cake? _____________________________?


Answer:

25/8 or 3 ¹/₈ cups

Step-by-step explanation:

This problem is a matter of ratio and proportion, in which two ratios are equal. We can express them in fractions,

1 ¹/₄ : 1  :: x : 2 ¹/₂

5/4 : 1 :: x : 5/2

(5/4) / 1 = x / (5/2)

(5/4) * (5/2) = x

25/8 = x

Therefore, we need 25/8 or 3 ¹/₈ cups of flour.
