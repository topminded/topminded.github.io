5. A mule pulls a cart of milk 10 meters with a force of 50 newtons. Calculate the work done by a mule.


PROBLEM

A mule pulls a cart of milk 10 meters with a force of 50 newtons. Calculate the work done by a mule.

SOLUTION

Work done is calculated using the formula force × distance. The force applied multiplied to the distance equals the work done.

[tex]f \: - 50 \: newtons \: [/tex]

[tex]w \: - f \times d[/tex]

[tex]w \: - 50n \times 10 \: meters[/tex]

[tex]w- 500 j \: (joules)[/tex]

Answer: 500 J
