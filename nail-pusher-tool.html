9. demonstrate the proper use of the following tools a. cuticle nail pusher b. nail file c. orangewood stick d. nail cutter​


Answer:

a. To use a cuticle nail pusher, first apply cuticle remover or soak your nails in warm water to soften the cuticles. Gently push back and scrape away the dead skin and cuticle from around the base of the nail using the curved end of the tool. Be careful not to push too hard or damage the delicate skin around the nail.

b. To use a nail file, determine the desired shape and length for your nails. Hold the file at one end and use a light back-and-forth motion to file the nails in one direction, working from the corners to the center. Repeat until the nails have the desired shape and a smooth edge.

c. To use an orangewood stick, first soften your cuticles by soaking your hands in warm water or applying cuticle remover. Use the pointed end of the stick to gently push back the edges of the cuticles and clean under the nails. You can also use the flat end to smooth the surface of the nails and remove any excess polish.

d. To use a nail cutter, first determine the desired length for your nails. Position the blades of the cutter at the base of the nail and squeeze the handles together to cut through the nail in a smooth, even motion. Be sure to only cut a small amount at a time to avoid cracking or splitting the nails. After cutting, use a nail file to shape and smooth the edges.
