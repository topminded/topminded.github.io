13. each batch of cookie mix needs 1.2 cups of sugar, and each batch can make 15 cookies. if Miya is making 5 batches of cookies, how many cups of sugar does she need?​


Answer:

If each batch of cookie mix needs 1.2 cups of sugar, then for 5 batches of cookies, the amount of sugar needed can be found by multiplying the amount of sugar needed for one batch by the number of batches:

5 batches x 1.2 cups/batch = 6 cups

Therefore, Miya needs 6 cups of sugar to make 5 batches of cookies.
