7. sunrise is to dawn as sunset is to​


The term "dawn" is synonymous with the start of morning twilight. "Sunrise" occurs the moment the disc of the sun peeks above the eastern horizon due to the Earth's rotation. "Sunset" is the opposite. It occurs the moment the disc of sun completely disappears below the western horizon.


8. The dawn of neutrophilia pertains to the:


Answer:

secondary granules

Explanation:

At the "dawn" of neutrophilia, these secondary granules are most obvious in the golgi area. As the cell matures closer to a metamyelocyte, they fill the entire cytoplasm. While the cytoplasm shifts to producing secondary granules it also looses the prominence of its primary granules.
