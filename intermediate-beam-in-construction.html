4. When it comes to the pouring of 4th level suspended concrete slab and beam, if you are the contractor of the construction project, which do you prefer, ordering concrete mix or using one bagger mixer?​


Answer:

Strength

One of the most important factors before ordering ready-mix concrete is knowing the required compressive strength. Typically, reinforced concrete ranging between 3,500 to 4,000 psi is used on footing and slabs on grade; between 3,500 to 5,000 psi on suspended slabs, beams and girders; and walls and columns normally require between 3,000 to 5,000 psi. When non-reinforced concrete is used different strengths will be required. If you are using it on footings and walls, a concrete strength of 2,500 psi could be enough while between 4,000 to 5,000 psi is needed for pavements. Outdoor concrete used on home improvements should be ordered with a strength ranging from 3,500 to 4,500 psi.

Aggregates

The maximum coarse aggregate must also be specified when ordering ready-mix concrete. If you use larger coarse aggregate in a cross-section where the steel ratio is high, then you could be in for big problems due to honeycombing. The maximum size coarse aggregate ordered must be determined by the American Concrete Institute Standard (ACI) and depends on the spacing of reinforcing steel, inside form dimensions and available aggregates

24. Choose the letter of the sentence that shows the correct sentence construction. Select one: a. Hughes Electronics can beam cable directlywith its direct TV to your home . b. Hughes Electronics can beam cable with its direct TV directly to your home . c. Hughes Electronics can beam cable directly to your home withits direct TV. d. Hughes Electronics with its direct TV can beam cable directly to your home .


Answer:

C. Hughes Electronic can beam cable directly to your home with its direct TV.

The sentence construction of the statement has the pattern of S-TV-IO-DO (subject-transitive verb-indirect object-direct object).

The subject is the main character of the statement and it is “Hughes Electronic”. The predicate is the action that the subject can do and it is “can beam cable”. It is classified as a transitive verb because the action has a receiver, which is the direct object. The indirect object is the one that determines to whom the action or the verb is done or referring to. The direct object answers the question what or whom. In the statement, the direct object is “with its TV” and the indirect object is “directly to your home”.

What can Hughes Electronic do? – “can beam cable” – To where? – “directly to your home” – Through what? – “with its direct TV”

26. 1. In a construction site, a crane is used to lift a 399-kg metal beam to a height of 10 meters in 0.75 minute. Calculate the power of the crane.​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The power of an object doing a work W in a given time t is given by

[tex]\boxed{P = \frac{W}{t}}[/tex]

where:

P = power

W = work

t = time

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the work

W = mgh

W = (399 kg)(9.8 m/s²)(10 m)

W = 39,102 J

Solving for the power

Note: 0.75 min = 45 s

[tex]P = \dfrac{W}{t}[/tex]

[tex]P = \dfrac{\text{39,102 J}}{\text{45 s}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{P = \text{868.93 W}}[/tex]

Therefore, the power of the crane is 868.93 W.
