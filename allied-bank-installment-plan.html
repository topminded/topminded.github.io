6. What are the factors to be considered in planning for the installation?​


The factors to be considered in planning for an installation depend on the type of installation being performed. Here are some general factors that should be considered:

1.)Purpose: The purpose of the installation should be clearly defined, including the intended use, audience, and goals.

2.)Site requirements: The location of the installation should be chosen based on factors such as space, power supply, lighting, temperature, and safety requirements.

3.)Equipment and materials: The necessary equipment and materials for the installation should be identified and procured, including hardware, software, and any other necessary components.

4.)Installation process: The process for installing the equipment and materials should be carefully planned, including any necessary assembly, configuration, testing, and troubleshooting.

5.)Personnel: The personnel responsible for the installation should be identified, including any necessary technical experts or support staff.

6.)Schedule: The schedule for the installation should be planned in advance, taking into account any necessary downtime or interruptions in service.

7.)Budget: The budget for the installation should be carefully considered, including any necessary expenses for equipment, materials, personnel, and other costs.

8.)Documentation: The installation process should be fully documented, including any necessary instructions, procedures, or manuals for future reference.

By considering these factors, the installation process can be carefully planned and executed to ensure a successful outcome.

11. Allied banking corporation vs lim sio wan


Allied Banking Corporation vs Lim Sio Wan

On November 14, 1983, Lim Sio Wan deposited PhP 1,152,597.35 in the Allied Banking Corporation bank as money market placement.

On December 5, 1983, Christina So, pretended to be a representative of Lim Sio Wan and withdrew the PhP 1,152,597.35 amount. Christina So deposited it in the account of Filipinas Cement Corporation (FCC) in Metrobank.

Lim Sio Wan went to Allied Banking Corporation bank and said she did not know a certain Christina So and did not authorize her to withdraw her money.

Lim Sio Wan sued Allied Bank. Allied Bank sued Metrobank and Metrobank sued Filipinas Cement Corporation (FCC).

In the end, Allied Bank ended up paying back Lim Sio Wan and all of the damages.

System installation planning

Last Updated: 2021-07-19

Thorough planning ensures that you have everything to meet all the prerequisites for the successful setup and installation of the system.

Planning minimizes errors during installation and enables a quicker upgrade or installation. This planning information helps you integrate the system into your data center, plan power and environmental needs, and prepare for any unique configurations you need to handle.

To ensure that planning is completed successfully, assign a planning project manager to provide a documented plan that includes the following items:

A timeline for the activities

Each major activity phase and the intended outcome

A list of responsibilities and the person assigned

A current system diagram and configuration listing. Include all hardware content, software content, cabling, and other pertinent configuration items, particularly if this is a modification to an existing system

Note: Give special attention to disk units, clustering, and logical partition (LPAR) system configurations.

A key contacts list, including off-hours contact information for all key task or activities participants

A plan for communicating appropriate elements of your plan with key personnel.

Completing a Technical Delivery Assessment (TDA) document is part of the purchasing process of Cloud Pak System. This document centralizes all the information that is needed to install your system.

Site-readiness planning

Use the site-readiness planning when preparing scenarios for a new installation, a model upgrade, hardware modifications, or software modifications.

Network configuration planning

As you plan for installing systems in your data center, review information about the network resources and your configuration options.

Hardware overview

Review the overall system layout and the physical location of each device in the system.
