14. Why technology integration is important in the teaching process?​


Answer:

Technology integration in the teaching process can enhance student learning by providing new and different ways for students to access information, express their understanding, and collaborate with their peers. Additionally, technology can help personalize learning, providing students with individualized instruction and resources. Furthermore, technology can help teachers to be more efficient and effective in their instruction, by providing tools for assessment, management, and communication. Overall, technology can help to create an engaging and interactive learning environment that can support student success.

6. Multicultural awareness and appreciation: This generation is more aware and accepting of other cultures, have knowledge of other countries and are open to exploring.
