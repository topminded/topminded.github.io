1. Ano ang National Irrigation Administration?


Ano ang National Irrigation Administration?

ANSWER : Ang National Irrigation Administration (NIA) ay isang korporasyong pagmamay-ari  at kontrolado ng pamahalaan (GOCC) na pangunahing responsable para sa pag-unlad na patubig sa pilipinas. Nilikha ito sa ilalim ng Republic Act (RA) 3601 noong Hunyo 22,1963. Ang Charter nito ay binago ulit ng Presidential Decree (PD) 552 noong ika-11 ng setyembre 1974 at PD 1702 noong Hulyo 17, 1980, kapwa ang pagtaas ng kapitalisasyon nito at pagpapalawak ng awtoridad nito. Ang tagapagpaunlad nito ay ang Irrigation Division ng wala na Bureau of Public Works. PD No.1, na may petsang setyembre 23,1972, isinama ang lahat ng mga aktibidad ng irrigasyon sa ilalim ng NIA.

6. the difference and similarities between national irrigation system from communal irrigation system​


Answer:

National irrigation systems (NIS) are large and medium schemes. These are basically operated and maintained by NIA where beneficiaries are charged irrigation service fee for the services rendered in the delivery of water.

Communal irrigation systems (CIS) are small-scale schemes and constructed with the participation of farmer-beneficiaries thru their IAs. ... Private irrigation systems are those constructed, operated and maintained by private individuals or groups with or without technical assistance by NIA or other government agencies.
