8. why it's positive globalization transportation, communication, culture, unity, technology?Why positive ang transportation, communication, and so on​


Globalization refers to the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of people, businesses, and governments around the world. This interconnectedness is largely driven by advances in transportation, communication, and technology, which have made it easier and faster to connect and exchange information, goods, and services across borders.

There are several ways in which globalization has had a positive impact. In the realm of transportation, for example, globalization has made it easier and more affordable for people to travel and access goods and services from other parts of the world. This has facilitated the growth of international trade and tourism, which have brought economic benefits to many countries and regions.

In the realm of communication, globalization has made it easier for people to connect and share ideas, knowledge, and experiences with others around the world. This has helped to break down cultural barriers and promote greater understanding and unity among people from different backgrounds.

In the realm of technology, globalization has facilitated the growth of the global economy and the development of new technologies and industries. This has created new jobs and opportunities for people around the world, and has helped to improve living standards and quality of life in many parts of the world.

Overall, globalization has had a positive impact on transportation, communication, culture, unity, and technology. It has facilitated the exchange of goods, ideas, and experiences among people from different parts of the world, and has helped to promote economic growth and development.
