10. A desktop computer is to be cooled by a fan whose flow rate is 0.30 m3 /min. determine the mass flow rate of air through the fan at an elevation of 3400 m where the air density is 0.7 kg/m3 . also, if the average velocity of air is not to exceed 95 m/min, determine the minimum diameter of the casing of the fan.


Answer:

63.5 mm pabrainliest po thanks

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Flow rate , V = 0.30m³/min

Density of air, p (rho) = 0.7 kg/m³

average velocity of air, v = 95m/min

Mass flow rate,

m = p V

m = 0.7 (0.3) = 0.21 kg/min

Flow rate can be written as,

V= A v

Where A is the cross section of the fan

0.30 = A (95)

0.30 = (π/4) d² (95)

d² = [0.30] / [(π/4)(95)]

d = 0.0634 m

d = 63.41 mm approximately equal to 63.5 mm

Answer:

1. 89 hr = 3 da & 17 hr

2. 870 s = 14 min & 30 s

4. 7, 990 min = 5 da, 13 hr & 10 min

5. 37 min = 2, 220 s

Step-by-step explanation:

1. 89 hr x (1 da/24 hr) = 3 da

* 3 da x (24 hr/1 da) = 72 hr

89 hr - 72 hr = 17 hr

2. 870 s x (1 min/60 s) = 14 min

* 14 min x (60 s/1 min) = 840 s

870 s - 840 s = 30 s

3. 47 hr x (60 s/1 hr) = 2, 820 min

4. 7, 990 min x (1 hr/60 min) x (1 da/24 hr) = 5 da

* 5 da x (24 hr/1 da) x (60 min/1 hr) = 7, 220 min

* 7, 990 min - 7, 220 min = 790 min

790 min x (1 hr/60 min) = 13 hr

* 13 hr x (60 min/1 hr) = 780 min

790 min - 780 min = 10 min

5. 37 min x (60 s/1 min) = 2, 220 s
