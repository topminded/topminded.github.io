25. what is force?when dowe use this?​


Answer:

A force is a push or pull upon an object resulting from the object's interaction with another object. Whenever there is an interaction between two objects, there is a force upon each of the objects.

In physics, a force is any interaction that, when unopposed, will change the motion of an object. A force can cause an object with mass to change its velocity, i.e., to accelerate. Force can also be described intuitively as a push or a pull. A force has both magnitude and direction, making it a vector quantity

When a force acts (pushes or pulls) on an object, it changes the object's speed or direction (in other words, makes it accelerate). The bigger the force, the more the object accelerates. When a force acts on an object, there's an equal force (called a reaction) acting in the opposite direction.Feb 26, 2020

26. A farmer rears a total of 875 bulls and chicken. The number of chicken is 24 times the number of bulls. How many bulls are there in farm?


Answer:

There are 35 bulls in the farm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem

Let us analyze the given problem above.

What is asked?

The number of bulls in the farm.

What are the given?

total number of bulls and chickens - 875

number of chickens - 24 times the number of bulls

What is the equation?

Let x be the number of bulls.

24x be the number of chickens.

x + 24x = 875

Solution:

x + 24x = 875

25x = 875

25x/25 = 875/25

x = 35

Final Answer:

There are 35 bulls in the farm.

Checking:

x + 24x = 875

35 + 24(35) = 875

35 + 840 = 875

875 = 875 ✔

30. how dowe multiply and divide the intergers


First, multiply or divide the numbers (don't think about the signs). After that, follow these rules:
1. If you multiply or divide integers with the same signs (positive and positive; negative and negative), the answer will be given the positive sign.
2. If you multiply or divide integers with different signs (positive and negative; negative and positive), the answer will be given the negative sign.

For example:
1. 4 x 4 = +16
2. -9 ÷ 3 = -3
3. -7 x -3 = +21 (or simply 21)
4. 9 ÷ -3 = -3
