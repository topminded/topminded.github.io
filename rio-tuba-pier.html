21. Rio and kim had the same amount of money at the start. When rio gave 300 the ratio of rio's mobey to kim's money became 2:3. How much money they have each at first?


At the start = same amoun of money:

Rio's money:x                        Kim's money: x

After Rio gave 300 to Kim, the ratio becomes 2:3.

(x - 300) : (x + 300) = 2: 3

3(x - 300) = 2 (x + 300)

3x - 900 = 2x + 600


3x - 2x = 600 + 900

x = 1,500

Rio and Kim had 1,500 each.

The tuba is the lowest-pitched musical instrument in the brass family. As with all brass instruments, the sound is produced by lip vibration – a buzz – into a mouthpiece with three to six valves and a broad bell typically facing upward. Let Rio's money be X, Kim's money be Y.

First situation: 

X=Y

When Php 300 was given;

X-300 and Y+300.

Since the ratio is 2:3,

3(X-300)=2(Y+300)

3X-900=2Y+600

3X-2Y= 600+900

Since X=Y,

3X-2X= X= 1500 

or,

3Y-2Y= Y= 1500.

∴ they both had Php 1500 each at first.

30. Ano ang kahalagahan ng pier


A pier is a raised structure in a body of water, typically supported by well-spaced piles or pillars. Bridges, buildings, and walkways may all be supported by piers. Their open structure allows tides and currents to flow relatively unhindered, whereas the more solid foundations of a quay or the closely spaced piles of a wharf can act as a breakwater, and are consequently more liable to silting. Piers can range in size and complexity from a simple lightweight wooden structure to major structures extended over 1600 metres. In American English, pier may be synonymous with dock.
