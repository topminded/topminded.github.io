MEAN, MEDIAN, MODE, RANGE

============================================

[tex]\large\sf{↬}[/tex] Order 25, 32, 34, 34, 35, 37, 41. Mean. Median. Mode. Range.

[tex]\small\mathcal{ \: \: GIVEN\: NUMBERS \: \: :}[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf{25, 32, 34, 34, 35, 37, 41}[/tex]

FINDING THE MEAN

[tex]\small\mathcal{ \: \: SOLUTION\:\&\: ANSWER \: \: :}[/tex]

Add all of the given numbers together and divide the sum by the total value of the given numbers. (Total value of the given numbers [tex]\large\tt{\longmapsto}[/tex] [tex]\sf{7}[/tex])

[tex]\large\tt{\underline{Adding}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf{25 +32 +34 + 34 + 35 + 37 + 41}[/tex]

[tex]\large{\boxed{\bold{=238}}}[/tex]

∴ The sum is 238.

[tex]\large\tt{\underline{Dividing}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf{238 \div 7}[/tex]

[tex]\large{\boxed{\bold{=34}}}[/tex]

∴ The quotient and the mean is 34.

FINDING THE MEDIAN

[tex]\small\mathcal{ \: \: SOLUTION\:\&\: ANSWER \: \: :}[/tex]

Arrange the given numbers from least to greatest (The given numbers are already arranged from least to greatest). Add the middle numbers (If there are two middle numbers) and divide the sum by the total number of middle numbers.

Since there is only one middle number among the given numbers, number [tex]\large{\boxed{\bold{34}}}[/tex] will be our median.

FINDING THE MODE

[tex]\small\mathcal{ \: \: SOLUTION\:\&\: ANSWER \: \: :}[/tex]

Our mode will be the number that appears the most in the given numbers. ([tex]\sf{25, 32, \red{34}, \red{34}, 35, 37, 41}[/tex])

∴ The mode is 34.

FINDING THE RANGE

[tex]\small\mathcal{ \: \: SOLUTION\:\&\: ANSWER \: \: :}[/tex]

Our range will be the difference between the largest and smallest number of the given numbers. (Largest number = 41, Smallest number = 25)

[tex]\large\sf{41 - 25}[/tex]

[tex]\large{\boxed{\bold{=16}}}[/tex]

∴ The range is 16.

___________

[tex]\large\sf{↬}[/tex] The mean is the result of dividing sum of the given numbers by the total number of given numbers.

[tex]\large\sf{↬}[/tex] The median is the number in the middle of the set of numbers. (If there are two middle numbers, then add the two middle numbers, and divide the sum by the total number of middle numbers).

[tex]\large\sf{↬}[/tex] The mode is the most frequently used number among the numbers given.

[tex]\large\sf{↬}[/tex] The range is the difference between the largest and smallest number of the given numbers.
