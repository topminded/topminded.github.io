21. C. Explain why walls made with hollow blocks needs to be plastered with cement and coated with paint​


Walls made with hollow blocks need to be plastered with cement and coated with paint for several reasons. The plaster provides a smooth, even surface for the paint to adhere to. It also helps to protect the blocks from water damage and mold growth. The paint helps to protect the wall from further damage, such as UV rays and extreme weather. Additionally, the paint helps to make the wall look more attractive and can help to hide any imperfections or blemishes on the wall.

25. sixteen construction workers can finish cementing a floor of a building in 3 hours. on a certain day, only 5 contruction workers are available for the job. how long it will take the 5 contruction workers to do the cementing job​


Answer:

It's 9 hours 36 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

When the number of workers increases, the time required will decrease.

When the number of workers decreases, the time required will increase.

Thus, the given proportion is inverse proportion.

Let m be the unknown time.

By principles of inverse proportion:

16 x 3 = 5 x m

Simplify

48 = 5m

Take the value in the numerator to the denominator

m = 48/5

m = 9.6 hours

m = 9 hours 36 minutes
