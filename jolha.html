22. Kevin, Jolas, and jeriel have some 20-centavo, 50-centavo, and 1-peso coins, respectively. Kevin has 5 times as many coins as jeriel. Jolas has half the number of coins that jeriel has. If jolas has 27 fewer coins than kevin, how much money do they have in all?​


Answer:

30.6 Pesos

Explanation:

Let:

Kevin = x

Jolas = y

Jeriel = z

Then:

5x = z

y/2 = z

x - 27 = x

Therefore:

x = 3

y = 30

z = 15

Total amount = 3(20 ¢) + 30(50 ¢) + 15(Php 1)

=1560/100 +15

= Php. 30.6
