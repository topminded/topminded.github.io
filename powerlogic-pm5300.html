Problem:

At a benefit show, special tickets cost P35.00 each while regular tickets cost P15.00. What was the total sales if 275 regular tickets and 120 special tickets were sold?

Given:

Special Tickets - P35.00

Regular Tickets - P15.00

Solution:

✓You need to multipy the number of regular tickets, 275, by the cost of the ticket, P15.00.

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: 275 \\ \underline{ \times \: \: \: \: 15} \\ \: \: \: 1375 \\ \underline{ \: \: \: \: 275 \: \: \: } \\ \: \: \: 412 5[/tex]

✓You need to multipy the number of special tickets, 120, by the cost of the ticket, P35.00.

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: 120 \\ \underline{ \times \: \: \: \: 35} \\ \: \: \: \: 600 \\ \underline{ \: \: \: 360 \: \: \: } \\ \: \: 4200[/tex]

✓The add the products to get the total sales.
