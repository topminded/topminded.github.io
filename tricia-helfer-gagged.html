2. Miguel and Tricia have a total of ₱80. After Miguel spent ₱5.25 on a packet of corn chips, he is left with 4 times as much money as Tricia. How much does Tricia have?


Miguel's money: x
Tricia's money: y

x + y = 80

x = 80-y  ⇒  Miguel's money

Find Tricia's money, y:

x - 5.25 = 4y

(80 - y) - 5.25 = 4y

74.75 = 4y + y

5y/5 = 74.75/5

y = Php  14.95  ⇒  Tricia's money

Miguel's money:
x = 80-y
x = 80 - 14.95
x = Php 65.05
