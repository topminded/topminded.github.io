22. Sample authorization letter for water reconnection


There are instances when the water service of a residential unit is disconnected due to non-payment of the bill, tampering, or acts of God.

When this happens, the affected unit owner can apply for reconnection, or if he/she is not available, he/she can send an authorization letter under the name of any members of the household.

Here’s a sample of an authorization letter for the reconnection of water service. You need to supply the personal information.

[Name of the water utility company]

[Address]

[Date]

[centered] AUTHORIZATION LETTER

Our water service was disconnected last [date] due to [reason]. I am requesting that you process my request for reconnection through my authorized representative, [name], who is my [relationship to representative] and is part of our household.

Our house is located at [complete address], and you can contact me via [landline or mobile number].

My representative has a copy of my identification card with my signature and water bill.

Hoping you can quickly grant our request.

Respectfully yours,

[Unit owner’s name and signature]

Optionally, you can include in the letter your preferred schedule on when you want the service provider to visit your house if needed.

Before asking your representative to visit the water utility’s office, make sure to contact them first for the requirements and fees, if any.
