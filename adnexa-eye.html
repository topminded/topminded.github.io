21. If a cow has two eyes , a monkey has two eyes , a pig has two eyes , how many eyes do we have?​


Answer:

We have two eyes.

Step-by-step explanation:

Math Logic

The problem above is an example of Math logic problems. Math logic requires logical thinking and reasoning based on the given statements to figure out the answer.

Let us analyze the given logic problem.

"If a cow has two eyes, a monkey has two eyes, a pig has two eyes, how many eyes do we have?"

Some would answer 6, adding all the number of eyes of cow, monkey and pig. But that is wrong.

The logic here is the question, "how many eyes do we have?". The answer is two since we only have two eyes. It is not asking for the total number of eyes.
