6. it is a type of protractor used to measure the vertebral rotation and rib humping that is seen in scoliosis


Answer:

THE SCOLIOMETER

Explanation:

A scoliometer is a type of protractor used to measure the vertebral rotation and rib humping that is seen in scoliosis. To use a scoliometer, the patient bends forward from the waist, and the examiner holds the scoliometer against the patient's back. The examiner then looks through the viewer to line up the markings on the scoliometer with the patient's spine. The amount of vertebral rotation and rib humping can then be read from the scoliometer.

Explanation:
