what is the posible question to tge fish vendor of having a fish port near in ther place.

To make fish available to consumers at the right time and in the right place requires an effective marketing system. Fishermen who catch fish by labouring overnight (from common-property water bodies) do not usually sell fish in retail markets[18]. At the break of day, they take their catches to places where Nikaries/Beparies, or retailers, meet them and bargain by the lot. At the landing point, the number of intermediaries is low. Only one or two intermediaries may approach a fisherman. Once bargaining has started, other intermediaries remain at a distance and wait for their turn to deal, should the first intermediary fail to obtain the fisherman's lot. If the first intermediary is unsuccessful, another steps in to bargain for the catch. Normally, the first Nikary/Paiker-retailer does not allow this to happen and secures the lot for himself. No open bidding exists in such a case. Therefore, the poor fisherman often falls prey to the Nikari/Bepari/Paiker-retailer's crude exploitations. A fisherman, as a seller, cannot negotiate favourable prices for himself mainly because:

he meets buyers (intermediaries) one at a time and at different times,

he cannot keep fish for a long time because the product is highly perishable,

he has no specific place to sit in the market to sell his fish.

explanations

Entry into the market is difficult for fishermen for many reasons, mainly because of strong non-cooperation and resistance from the Paikers/retailers. Thus, it is obvious why fishing communities remain poor or are getting poorer over the years, although they trade an important, necessary and every-day commodity. Markets at the primary catch stage are almost completely non-competitive and therefore, exploitation is high.

12. Paano nakakatulong ang pag set up ng mga cold storage facility at fish port, sa mga mangingisda?


Ang pagkakaroon ng cold storage facility at fish port ay nakakatulong sa mga mangingisda sa pagpapreserba ng kanilang mga produkto, mas magandang kalagayan para sa kanila, at mas magandang oportunidad para sa kanilang negosyo. Ito ay isang magandang hakbang upang matulungan ang mga mangingisda na magkaroon ng mas magandang kabuhayan at magbigay ng mas magandang serbisyo sa kanilang mga mamimili.

Ang pag-set up ng mga cold storage facility at fish port ay nakakatulong sa mga mangingisda dahil nagbibigay ito ng magagandang pagkakataon sa mga mangingisda upang magkaroon ng mas mahabang oras upang makagawa ng mga produkto na may mas mahusay na kalidad at naipaparating ito sa mas mataas na presyo. Maaari silang magamit din upang maipagamit ang kanilang pagkakahinog na produkto sa mas mahabang oras at kahit na sa mga lugar na mas malayo. Bukod pa, ang mga cold storage facilities at fish port ay nakakatulong din sa pagpapabuti ng seguridad sa produkto at sa pagtitiyak na ito ay walang mapanganib na organismo na may epekto sa kalusugan.

24. a. Serial Portb. Ethernet Portc. USB Portd. DVI Porte. FireWire Portf. Parallel Port definnition ​


A. Serial Port - a connector by which a device that sends data one bit at a time may be connected to a computer.

B. Ethernet Port - Ethernet is a family of wired computer networking technologies commonly used in local area networks, metropolitan area networks and wide area networks.

C. USB Port - Stands for "Universal Serial Bus." USB is the most common type of computer port used in today's computers.

D. DVI Port - The Digital Virtual Interface is used to connect a video source, such as a video display controller, to a display device, such as a computer monitor.

E. FireWire Port - an interface standard for a serial bus for high-speed communications and isochronous real-time data transfer.

F. Parallel Port - a connector for a device that sends or receives several bits of data simultaneously by using more than one wire.
