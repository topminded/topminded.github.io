Answer:

In perhaps their most basic uses, stairs and steps share a single meaning. Both refer to a series of steps—those "structures consisting of a riser and a tread," as the definition of step puts. When they're outside, they're more often called 'steps' than they are called 'stairs.

“Steps” are individual risers in a “flight of stairs” or in a stair.

A stair is an arrangement of steps that will allow a person to go from a lower to a higher floor, or from a higher to a lower floor.

17. The slide makes a right triangle with the ground and the stairs. The “rise” is 1 meter shorter than the “run” and the slide is 1 meter longer than the “run.” Find the three sides of the slide.


Answer:

The sides are 3, 4, and 5 meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

The sides of the triangle formed by the stairs, slide (hypotenuse), and the ground:

Run = the distance formed along the ground between the bottoms of stairs and slideRise = the stairs connecting the ground and the upper end of slidesSlide = the slide, slanted side or hypotenuse of the triangle formed

Represent the sides of the triangle formed:

Run: xRise: x - 1   (one meter shorter than the run)Slide x + 1 (one meter longer than the run)

The three sides are represented by expressions (x), (x-1), and (x+1).

To solve for x:

Use the Pythagorean Theorem where the square of the hypotenuse of the right triangle is equal to the sum of the square of the two other sides.

(Hypotenuse)² = (side 1)² + (side 2)²(slide)² = (run)² + (rise)²

Equation:

(x + 1)² = (x)² + (x-1)²

x² + 2x + 1 = x² + x² - 2x + 1

x² + 2x + 1 = 2x² - 2x + 1

Move the right equation to the left side of equation, then equation the result to 0:

2x² - x² -2x - 2x + 1 - 1 = 0

x² - 4x = 0

Factor the equation (x² - 4x =0):

(x) (x-4) = 0

Equate each factor to 0:

x = 0

x - 4 = 0

x = 4

The sides are:

Run:  x = 4 metersRise: x-1 = (4-1) = 3 metersSlide: x+1 = (4)+1 = 5 meters

The sides are 3, 4, and 5 meters.

Check:

Pythagorean Theorem: The square of the hypotenuse of the right triangle is equal to the sum of the square of the two other sides.

Answer:

6 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = side of smaller square

Let 2x = side of larger square

Therefore, we can create an equation (x)² + (2x)² = 45

Solving for x:

(x)² + (2x)² = 45

x² + 4x² = 45

5x² = 45

x² = 9

x = 3

Side Length of Larger Square = 2x = 2(3) = 6 cm
