Answer:

My answers for the two questions are:

a. "Imagine that I am a customer. How can you sell the tissue box to me?"

Hello, thank you for considering our product. This tissue box is not just a regular tissue box. It is made of high-quality material that is soft and durable. It is perfect for people who are looking for a reliable and practical solution for their daily needs. You can use it at home, in the office, or even in your car. It also has a modern and sleek design that can blend in with any interior design.

b. "Using your imagination, show me five things that I can do with the tissue box."

Sure, here are five things you can do with this tissue box:

1.You can use it to store your makeup brushes and other beauty products.

2.You can cut out the sides and bottom to make a small drawer to store small items like jewelry, keys, or coins.

3.You can cover it with wrapping paper and use it as a gift box.

4.You can turn it into a piggy bank by cutting a small slot at the top and covering it with a piece of paper or tape.

5.You can make a DIY project by covering it with fabric and using it as a decorative storage box for your household items.

After my performance, I asked for feedback from my interviewer. They said that I did a good job highlighting the features and benefits of the tissue box and that my creativity in showing different ways to use it was impressive. I agree with their feedback and appreciate their positive comments.

To do better, I could have focused more on the specific needs of the customer and tailored my pitch to address those needs. I could also have practiced my presentation to make it more confident and engaging.
