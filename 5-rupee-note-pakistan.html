23. Veer space 15% of his monthly income in house rent and 60% of rest of household expenses if he says rupees 2200 10 rupees what is the total monthly income​


Step-by-step explanation:

Let Veer's monthly salary be 'x'.

So, income spent on the house rent -

= 15% of total

= 15/100 × x

= 15x/100

And, income spent on household expenses -

= 60% of total

= 60/100 × x

= 60x/100

Also, monthly saving = 2210 Rs.

Now,

Total income = money spent on house rent + money spent on household expenses + savings

=> x = 15x/100 + 60x/100 + 2210

=> x - 15x/100 - 60x/100 = 2210

=> (100x - 15x - 60x)/100 = 2210

=> 25x/100 = 2210

=> x = 2210 × 100/25

=> x = 2210 × 4

=> x = 8840

Hence Veer's total monthly income is 8,840 rupees

What is asked?

How much did he pay?

What are given?

number of hours - 3 hours

What is the number sentence?

We have two ways in finding the total amount that Vivek paid for renting a bike, first is by using addition and the second way is by using multiplication and addition.

Just remember the cost for the first hour and the cost for every 30 mins. He rented for 3 hours, so the next two hours is equal to 120 minutes or four 30 mins.

Rs 7 + (Rs 3 + Rs 3 + Rs 3 + Rs 3) = N

= Rs 7 + Rs 12
