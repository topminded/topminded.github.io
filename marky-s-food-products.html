23. marky is 14 cm taller than prince if the sum of their height is 249 cm find the height of each boy (true answer brainliest)⭐(wrong answer block)​


Answer:

Prince's height is 117.5 cm, while Marky's height is 131.5 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Let [tex]P[/tex] be Prince's height in cm. If Marky is 14 cm taller than Prince, then Marky's height can be represented as [tex](P+14)[/tex] cm.

Since the sum of their height is equal to 249 cm, we have that

[tex]\textsf{Prince's height}+\textsf{Marky's height}=249[/tex]

[tex]P+(P+14)=249[/tex]

Combine like terms and solve for [tex]P.[/tex]

[tex]P+P+14=249[/tex]

[tex]2P+14=249[/tex]

[tex]2P+14-14=249-14[/tex]

[tex]2P=235[/tex]

[tex]\therefore \boxed{P=\dfrac{235}{2} \ \textsf{cm}=117.5 \ \textsf{cm}}[/tex]

So, Prince's height is [tex]\frac{235}{2}[/tex] cm or [tex]117.5[/tex] cm (in decimal). Now, plug in the value of [tex]P[/tex] to [tex](P+14)[/tex] to determine Marky's height.

[tex]\textsf{Marky's height}=P+14[/tex]

[tex]\textsf{Marky's height}=\dfrac{235}{2}+14[/tex]

[tex]\therefore \boxed{\textsf{Marky's height}=\dfrac{263}{2} \ \textsf{cm}=131.5 \ \textsf{cm}}[/tex]

Therefore, Prince's height is 117.5 cm, while Marky's height is 131.5 cm.
