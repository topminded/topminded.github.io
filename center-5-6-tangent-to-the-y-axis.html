12. A circle is tangent at a y axis and center at (-2,-8)


Answer:

The equation of the circle is (x + 2)² + (y + 8)² = 4

Center (h, k): (-2, -8)

Radius: 2

Step-by-step explanation:

If the circle is tangent to the y-axis with center (h, k) at (-2, -8), then the point of tangency is (0, - 8). The radius is 2 units from the y-axis to x-coordinate (h) or the center.

To determine the equation of the circle:

(x - h)² + (y - k)² = r²

Center (h, k): (-2, -8)

Radius: 2

Substitute:

(x -(-2))² + (y -(-8))² = 2²

(x + 2)² + (y + 8)² = 4
