Pantene, on the other hand, is a brand of hair care products that includes shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. Clear and Head and Shoulders shampoos are two products that promise to eliminate dandruff from our hair. In order to know which one is the most convenient according to each person’s criteria, we have elaborated a comparison between both shampoos so that people can better differentiate between them and draw their own conclusions. If we compare Head and Shoulders vs Clear shampoos, it will be easier to understand what each one has to offer.  Head and Shoulders is a shampoo that promises to eliminate dandruff from the hair, while also promising to care for the hair from an aesthetic point of view. This product has variants to hydrate, nourish and repair hair. Head and Shoulders claims to care for hair deeply, while the other brands only remove dandruff. The brand has shampoos exclusively for men, arguing that the male scalp is very different from the female one. It also has a variant that is shampoo and conditioner at the same time, which serves to save people time when washing their heads. Clear is a shampoo that, like Head and Shoulders, promises to eliminate dandruff from hair, while taking care of it aesthetically. It also has a variant that is shampoo and conditioner at the same time. The white shampoo containers are for women and the blue ones are for men. The Clear shampoo fights the itching caused by dandruff, fights the dryness of the hair, and reduces hair loss due to hair weakness. Clear fights annoying symptoms of dandruff such as irritation and burning when a person scratches his/her head because he/she has dandruff. Head and Shoulders vs Clear: Effectiveness Both shampoos are effective against dandruff. Although they each do it differently. Head and Shoulders emphasizes the beauty of hair while fighting dandruff. In contrast, Clear shampoo is more effective in combating the annoying symptoms of dandruff such as itching, dryness, burning, and irritation. All Head and Shoulders shampoos moisturize hair with dandruff, whichbenefits the balance that hair should maintain despite having dandruff. All variants of Clear shampoo deeply cleanse the scalp, as this brand believes that this is the most important thing to combat dandruff. Neither brand states how long it takes to remove dandruff from the hair. Although, on average, both Head and Shoulders and Clear take two weeks to eliminate this problem. Both brands generate shine in the hair, in addition to fighting dandruff. When it comes to eliminating dandruff from the hair, it is recommended that the shampoo fights the symptoms of this problem. And that it takes care of the aesthetics of the hair, prevents the breakage of the hair and finally eliminates dandruff definitively. Final Words on Head and Shoulders vs Clear When considering which one to go for, you should look at multiple factors to help you decide.
