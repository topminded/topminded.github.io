4. what would you do if your loved one or somebody close to you is a victim of a heinous crime​


Answer:

How can you help a loved one, who has become a victim of crime?

   If you only suspect that your loved one has become a victim of crime, you can subtly ask, is everything alright? You can also tell him/her about your observations in his/her behaviour.    If your loved one tells you about his/her experience of crime, give him/her encouraging feedback on the fact that he/she has dared to talk about it.    If it is a case of domestic violence, tell him/her about the possibility of a restraining order, if necessary, and prepare a safety plan together.    Do not be fooled by the victim’s normal and quite efficient behaviours. It is common that during the first few days after the crime, the experience does not feel like much. Shock protects the human mind from stressful emotions. Usually after a few days, or within a few weeks, the victim begins to react to what has happened.    Do not wait for a certain reaction. The way people react varies a lot.    Be present and ready to help and refer the victim, if necessary, to professional help.    Tell the victim that it is a case of a crime and that it would be in his/her best interest to report the crime. The quicker the police are informed, the easier the crime is to be investigated and the victim’s safety can be secured in the future.

26. Listen to the following songs and identify its emotion.SOMEONE YOU LOVED by: Lewis CapaldiIMAHE by: Magnus HavenVICTIMS OF LOVE​


Answer:

Although all songs share sad emotions, it can be noted that each of the songs reflect situations that brought the speakers varying degrees of sadness.

Explanation:

Someone You Loved

The speaker talks about being alone and how much he would want to have someone to be with him. Associating nighttime with feeling of abandonment, he calls out the person who isn’t there with anymore.

Imahe

This song is reminiscent of those that would talk about having the right love at the wrong time, or having a lover tagged as the one that got away. The speaker keeps going back to the moment he and his girl had to part ways to give in to other priorities.

Victims of Love

The song describes a relationship that seems to have gone through battle with both lovers becoming victims in the end. The speaker tells how from being easy and understanding, they ended up giving up and losing the will to hold on to their love.
