The cost of each pair of pants.

number of pairs of pants bought - 4

total cost of T-shirts and pants - P 5000.00

cost of a T-shirt - P 250.00

What operation will be used?

Multiplication, Division and Subtraction

What is the number sentence?

(P 5000.00 - 6 × P 250.00) ÷ 4 = N

25. Salina used 2/3 of a string to tie up a parcel and 1/2 of the remainder to tie up a box. If the fraction of the string left was 60 cm long, what is the original length of the string?


Let x= length of string,

[tex]x - \frac{2}{3} x - \frac{1}{2} ( \frac{1}{3} x) = 60cm \\ x - \frac{2x}{3} - \frac{x}{6 } = 60cm \\ x - \frac{4x}{6} - \frac{x}{6 } = 60cm \\ x - \frac{5x}{6} = 60cm \\ \frac{x}{6} = 60cm \\ x = 360cm \: length \: of \: string[/tex]
