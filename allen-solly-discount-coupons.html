13. What relationship between the required return and the coupon interest rate will cause a bond to sell at a discount?


When the needed return is higher than the coupon rate, a bond sells at a discount. When the needed return is lower than the coupon rate, a bond sells for a premium. When the necessary return and the coupon rate are identical, a bond sells for its face value.
