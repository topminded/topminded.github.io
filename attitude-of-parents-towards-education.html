1. Perceptions of the value of studying the Humanities and Social Sciences: Students could investigate the attitudes and beliefs of students, parents, and educators towards the HUMSS strand or other strands that focus on social sciences and humanities subjects.PR1 SUBJECT EXPLAIN IT​


The statement suggests that students can research and analyze the perceptions of different groups, including students, parents, and educators, regarding the value of studying Humanities and Social Sciences. In particular, the focus is on the HUMSS strand, which is a strand in the senior high school curriculum that emphasizes social sciences and humanities subjects. The purpose of the investigation is to gain insight into the attitudes and beliefs of different stakeholders towards this field of study, which could inform educational policies and programs.

The study could involve conducting surveys or interviews with students, parents, and educators to understand their perceptions of the value of studying the Humanities and Social Sciences. The findings could reveal common misconceptions or gaps in understanding that need to be addressed. Additionally, it could provide valuable insights into how to promote the value and importance of studying social sciences and humanities subjects, which could potentially increase enrollment in these courses.

Overall, the statement highlights the importance of understanding the attitudes and beliefs of different stakeholders towards the Humanities and Social Sciences, which could ultimately lead to better educational policies and programs that support this field of study.
