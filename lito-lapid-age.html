15. lito is three times sandra's age. the difference of their ages is 10 find their present ages. URGENT


Answer:

Sandra = 5, Lito = 15

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:  

Sandra's Age = x

Lito's Age      = 3x

But the difference is 10, so add 10 to the least one (which is Sandra's Age)

we get,

x+10 = 3x

10 = 2x

x = 5 (Sandra's Age)

3x = 15 (Lito's Age)

Answer:

Lito = 15 years old

Sandra = 5 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be Sandra's age

and 3x (thice the age of Sandra) be Lito's age

Difference of their ages = 10

Lito's age - Sandra's age = 10

3x - x = 10

2x = 10

x = 5

Substitute:

Sandra's age ⇒ x = 5 years old

Lito's age ⇒ 3x = 3(5) = 15 years old

30. lito is 12 years older than this wife. if we combine lito's age and his wife, the sum is 60. what is the age of lito's wife?


sum of their age is 60
lito is 12 yrs older, therefore lito's wife is 48 yrs old

Let X be the age of lito's wife.
X+12 be lito's age since he is 12 yrs older than his wife.
We add these equations and equate it to 60.
X+X+12=60
Combine like terms and transpose 12 to the right.
2X=48
Divide both sides by 2
X=24
So, lito's wife is 24 yrs old.
To find lito's age we just add 12 to 24 , lito is 36 yrs old
