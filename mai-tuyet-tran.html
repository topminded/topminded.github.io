19. Which has the greatest molar heat of combustion? trans-1,2-dimethylcyclopentane trans-1,3-dimethylcyclopentane methylcyclohexane cycloheptane cis-1,2-dimethylcyclopentane


Isomers are hydrocarbon compounds that have the same molecular formula but different structures. Of these options, the highest molar heat of combustion is cycloheptane.

Detail Explanation

Every organic compound that has carbon and hydrogen or is often called a carbon compound has several isomers such as geometric isomers. These geometric isomers occur when the same compound is found but the alkyl groups can be rotated and swapped places. Molar heat is also affected by the boiling point of combustion or reaction. The straighter the chain, the greater or higher the molar heat obtained during the combustion reaction.

Hydrocarbon compounds that have geometric isomers have the same molar heat. The molar heat of organic compounds or carbon that has straight chains will be greater than that of hydrocarbon compounds that have branches.

After getting the explanation, we can answer the question. Of these options, the compound with the highest or largest molar heat is cycloheptane.

21. are trans-women women?


Answer:

Yes, trans women are women. A trans woman is a person who was assigned male at birth based on their physical anatomy, but identifies and lives as a woman. Gender identity is a deeply felt sense of one's own gender, and it may not always align with the sex assigned at birth. Therefore, it is important to respect a person's gender identity and use the pronouns and language that aligns with their gender identity.
