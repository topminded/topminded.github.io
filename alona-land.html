Solution/Proof:

*** NO NO NO to GUESS GUESS GUESS THE ANSWER! ***

Let:

x = present age of Yvonne

a = present age of Alona

Conditions:

a = 2x --> twice as Yvonne's age

but a = 2x, so

a + 15 = 49

2x + 15 = 49

therefore the answer is letter A. 2x + 15 = 49

To solve for Yvonne's current age, lets solve for x,

2x + 15 = 49

2x = 49 - 15

2x = 34

x = 34/2

x = 17

Therefore Yvonne's current age is 17 years old

To solve for Alona's current age, let's solve for a,

a = 2x

a = 2(17)

a = 34

Therefore Alona's current age is 34 years old.

25. 7. Alona and Adora have a combined weight of 118.42kilograms. If Alona is heavier by 8.06 kilograms, howmuch do each of them weigh ?​


Answer:

Adora: 55.18 kgAlona: 63.24 kg

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be Adora's weight and x + 8.06 be Alona's.

Find Adora's weight:

[tex] \\ \sf{}(x) + (x + 8.06) = 118.42[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{}x + x + 8.06 = 118.42[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{}2x + 8.06 = 118.42[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{}2x = 118.42 - 8.06[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{}2x = 110.36[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{}x = \frac{110.36}{2} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \large\boxed{ \sf{} x = 55.18} \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

Find Alona's weight:

[tex]\\ \sf{} = 55.18 + 8.06[/tex]

[tex]\\ \large\boxed{\sf{} = 63.24} \\ \\ \\ [/tex]
