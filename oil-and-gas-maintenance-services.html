5. when can you apply preventive maintenance in computer hardware servicing​


Answer:

Every three to six months you should do a thorough cleaning of your entire hardware system. You can do this in a number of ways, including with a computer vacuum. You'll want to save and close any active files before you unplug your device to begin cleaning.

The goals of preventative maintenance are to reduce the likelihood of hardware failures, extend the useful life of the system, minimize system crashes caused by outdated drivers and other software problems, secure the system against viruses and other malware, and prevent data loss.
