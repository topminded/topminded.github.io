bones-bones is tough and rigid form of connective tissues. it is the weight bearing organ of human body and it is responsible for almost all strength of human skeleton.

associated cartilages-cartilages is also a form of connective tissues but it is not tough as rigid as the bone. the main difference between the cartilages and the bone is minaralization facto. bones are mineralized with calciumsalts while cartilages are not.

joints-joints are important components of human skeleton because they make the human skeleton mobile. a joints occurs between two or more bones and cartilages.

the main functions of the skeletal system are;

protection-shape-support and movement
