10. includes information on how your project may contribute to the mission and vision of the funding agency


There is information in the introduction about how your project might fit into the agency's mission and vision.

The first section, the Introduction, identifies how and where the applicant’s mission and the\sfunder’s mission intersect or align. It tells the funder why they should give money to projects in that general area. It also talks about the applicant's partners and explains why they want to be a part of the project.

Give information about the funding agency to show that you understand the mission.Say what the mission of the organization the proponents work for is and how it fits with the mission of the funding agency. Also say when the proponents' organization was founded, what its biggest achievements are, and anything else that shows it can do the proposed project.Show and talk about the other partner agencies and why they are good ones.Tell the funding agency why they should support the project.

Before a full proposal is sent to a funder, it is common for them to ask for a one- to five-page concept paper. In the government contracting sector, these concept papers are sometimes called "white papers." This saves them time because it gets rid of ideas that aren't likely to get money. When funders ask for concept papers, they usually give a template or format.

14. It includes the mission and vision and brief overview of a project or concept paper​


Vision is the ideals or dreams of an organization or company to be achieved in the future by all members, expressed in a simple but inspiring form, general and philosophical in nature, made through a deep reflection and preferably the aspirations of the organization.

Mission is the basic reason for the existence of an organization and is an affirmation and elaboration of the vision which is a statement of what must be done and can be an outline of the path by the organization in achieving the vision.      

Project visions and missions are the visions and missions of the construction company that have been modified according to the objectives, personnel, and existing conditions of the project without leaving the essence / philosophy of the vision and mission of the parent company.

The project visions and missions after being formulated by the project leader must be disseminated to all lines of the organization even to the lowest level.

Socialization is not only conveying the vision and mission sentences, but also the meaning, taste, and spirit of the visions and missions, which of course is not an easy matter. It takes a high degree of trust from team members to understand, accept, believe, and make it the foundation or direction of the organization.

Some sample projects:

Construction Engineering Project

In its activities, the most dominant activities carried out in this project are feasibility studies, engineering design, procurement and construction.

Engineering project Manufacturing

Broadly speaking, the activities of this project include all activities that are to produce new products.

Project Management Services

In the process, the main activities in this project are designing management information systems, designing efficiency and savings programs, diversification, mergers and acquisitions, providing emergency assistance to disaster-affected areas, designing strategies to reduce crime and the use of illegal drugs and others.
