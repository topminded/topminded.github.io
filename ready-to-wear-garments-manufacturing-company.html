PROBLEMS IN READY-TO-WEAR GARMENT MANUFACTURING COMPANY

The operator's or the employees' absenteeism is one of the issues that can arise in the production of ready-to-wear clothing. Employee absences will worsen the manpower shortage in the sewing department, which could postpone the manufacture of ready-to-wear clothing and delay the delivery of orders to customers by the predetermined date.

HOW TO SOLVE THE IDENTIFIED PROBLEM USING DECISION MAKING PROCESS?The manufacturing company management can solve the problem by means of the following process:

1. First the management should define first the reason why the employees are absent frequently. This is the first thing that the management should do in order for them to decide to address the problem.

2. After gathering the problem, the next step is the management should gather information based on facts and data. The management will ask themselves what are the things that they need to know in order to make the right decision.

3. After the management should know the reason of absenteeism this is the time that  they need to identify alternatives in order to determine which course of action is the best way to solve the delay. For example if the employees problem is salary, the management should motivate their employees by means of monetary compensation, or remuneration or by means of giving bonuses.

4. Then after identifying alternatives, they will choose among the alternatives, they should take action and review their final decision. If the problem of the employees is monetary therefore they should give them monetary rewards in order to motivate to work and prevent absent in their work.
