What are the roles of Consultative Group to Assist the Poor ( CGAP ) in the field of microfinance institutions?

Its current objective is to support the development of financial systems that work for the poor, by improving the capacity of microfinance institutions to deliver flexible, high-quality financial services to the poor on a sustainable basis.

Four major activities during Phases I and II have been: (1) disseminating microfinance best practices, (2) grants to selected microfinance institutions (MFIs) to spur learning and innovation, (3) mainstreaming microfinance within member donor agencies, and (4) fostering national policy environments conducive to microfinance.
