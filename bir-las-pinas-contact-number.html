14. What is the time signature of las piñas hymn


Answer:

What is the time signature of Las Piñas hymn?

[tex] \frac{4}{4} [/tex]

What is time signature?

The time signature tells you the number of beats or counts in a measure as well as the type of note that fills one beat. A time signature contains two numbers, the top number tells you how many beats there are in a measure or bar of a song. The bottom number tells you what kind of note its beat.

For example, in a 4/4 time signature each measure contains 4 quarter notes. The top 4 tells us that there are 4 beats in each measure and the bottom 4 tells us what note value constitutes one beat. In this case, a quarter note gets one beat.

28. What is the Hornbostel-Sachs of Las Piñas Bamboo Organ?​


Answer:

If there’s one thing that is unique about Las Piñas, it is the Bamboo Organ, which is considered a Philippine National Treasure. On November 24, 2003, it was given this recognition by the National Museum of the Philippines since “it is the only 19th century Bamboo Organ in the Philippines that has survived and is still functioning.” This unique musical instrument is composed of 1,031 pipes, and 902 of which are made out of bamboo while the rest are metal.
