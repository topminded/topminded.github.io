26. what is irrigation design can be in slopping land to reduce erosiona. furrow irrigationb.sprinkler irrigationc.surface irrigationd.rotavator​


Answer:

A. Furrow irrigation

Explanation:

Furrows are small, parallel channels, made to carry water in order to irrigate the crop. The crop is usually grown on the ridges between the furrows.

Furrow irrigation is also suited to the growing of tree crops. In the early stages of tree planting, one furrow alongside the tree row may be sufficient but as the trees develop then two or more furrows can be constructed to provide sufficient water. Sometimes a special zig-zag system is used to improve the spread of water

Answer:

Sprinkler irrigation

Explanation:

It's a method of applying irrigation water which is similar to natural rainfall. Water is distributed through a system of pipes usually by pumping. It is then sprayed into the air through sprinklers so that it breaks up into small water drops which fall to the ground.
