27. an element of design that tells something about the contour or figure of the person​


Answer and Explanation

There are three answers to this:

Shape, Form, and Silhouette

In Elements of Art, the Form and/or Shape creates a solid image of the subject or person. If a color is added within the Shape or Form it creates a Silhouette. In character design, the silhouette is important because it is the shape or form of a character that is easily recognizable by the viewer. For example, a silhouette of Son Goku (Dragon Ball) can easily be recognized because of the form/shape of his hair.
