9. A client's procurement system ends with the assumption of a liability and the eventual payment of the liability.


Because accounts payable are not considerably underestimated, a client's procurement system concludes with the assumption of a liability and the eventual payment of the liability.

Streamlining an organization's procurement process and achieving desired objectives while saving money, cutting back on time, and developing beneficial supplier relationships are all examples of procurement procedures, structured methods, and ways. The source-to-settle cycle is completed by the procurement management process by integrating with accounts payable and supplying supporting documents to aid in the processing of supplier invoices for payment. Transactional purchases of products and services are also a part of this process.

The term procurement process refers to the sequence of procedures required to get goods or services from a request to an approved purchase order and invoice. Although the terms procurement and purchase are often used interchangeably, they have some subtle differences. While procurement refers to the activities involved in acquiring them, purchasing refers to the overall process of acquiring necessary commodities and services on behalf of an organization. An organization's procurement procedure is particular to its operations and surroundings.

28. what is prepaid expense? TAGALOG!


Ano ang Prepaid Expense

Ang prepaid expense ay isang uri bayarin na binayaran mo ng mas maaga o "in advance" bago mo pa gamitin ang isang bagay / produkto o serbisyo.

Ang prepaid expense ay karaniwang tinatawag na asset ng isang kompanya, business o ng isang indibidwal dahil ito ay gagamitin para sa hinaharap o sa mga darating na araw.

Mga Halimbawa ng Prepaid Expense

Narito ang ilang mga halimbawa ng prepaid expense upang maintindihan ng maliwanag:

Insurance

Halos lahat ng klase ng insurance ay binabayaran ng advance. Gaya ng insurance ng sasakyan, binabayaran ang kabuuan ng isang taon o buwan in advance bago pa gamitin ang sasakyan.

Renta o Upa

Kung may inuupahan ka at nagbabayad ka ng maaga, gaya ng pagbayad sa unang araw ng buwan para sa isang buwan na upa ay tinatawag na prepaid expense dahil maaga ang iyong pagbayad bago mo pa magamit.

Cellphone Load

Bumili ka ng load pero gagamitin mo ito hindi lang sa arw ng iyong pagbili kundi pati na rin sa mga sumusunod na araw.
