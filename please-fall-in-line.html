WHAT IS DECLARATIVE SENTENCES, EXCLAMATORY SENTENCES, IMPERATIVE SENTENCES, AND INTERROGATIVE SENTENCES?

» DECLARATIVE is a sentence that makes a statement or simple sentence that contains a details or infos.

For example

Zalexa is a hot tempered girl.I like spicy foods.She loves to eat.

» EXCLAMATORY is a sentence that contains exclamations that tells a strong emotions. (!)

For example

How dare you!Ouch!I'm so mad at him!Don't talk to me, get out of my place!

» IMPERATIVE this sentence is a grammatical mood that forms a command or request.

For example

Order : Please be patient.Warning : Get out of that place!Instructions : Listen to your teachers carefully.

» INTERROGATIVE sentences asks a question.

For example

Where do you live?Which food would make you happy?Where are you going?#CarryOnLearning
