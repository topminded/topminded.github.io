The factors of 48 and 72 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 24, 48 and 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 9, 12, 18, 24, 36, 72 respectively. There are 3 commonly used methods to find the GCF of 48 and 72 - long division, prime factorization, and Euclidean algorithm.
