28. 2 points What is the surface area of a rectangular pyramid with the following dimensions: Length of rectangular base: 11 dm; Width of rectangular base: 8 dm; slant Height of triangular face: 9 dm * ng 9 dm 8 dm 11 dm 0 234 dm 430 dm 0259 dm


Answer:

259 dm²

Without memorizing any formula, let's try to come up with a solution.

The total surface area is simply add all the surface areas of each figures.

1st solve for the two triangles with b=11 and h=9

note: the slant height becomes the height or altitude of the triangle.

Area of ∆ = ½bh

Area of ∆ = ½(11)(9)

Area of ∆ = 49.5

there's 2∆ with this dimensions, so total is

49.5 × 2 = 99 dm²

now solve for the other two triangles with b=8 and h=9

Area of ∆ = ½bh

Area of ∆ = ½(8)(9)

Area of ∆ = 36

there's 2∆ with this dimensions, so total is

36 × 2 = 72 dm²

finally we solve the area of the base or the floor, it's a rectangle with Length = 11 and Width = 8. So let's solve for its area,

Area = L × W

Area = 11 × 8

Area = 88 dm²

So total surface area of the shape are the sum of the individual surface areas we solved above,

Total Surface Area = 99 + 72 + 88

Total Surface Area = 259 dm²

And there we solved the total surface area without memorizing any formulas.
