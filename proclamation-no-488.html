Since 9 is greater than or equal to 5, we need to round up the digit in the hundredth thousand place to the next digit, which is 3.

Therefore, rounding 2,597,488 to the nearest hundredth thousand gives us 2,600,000.

23. Calculate the mass in 488 mol of calcium carbonate, CaCO3


[tex]\large \bold {SOLUTION}[/tex]

Molar Mass of Ca = 40.1 u

Molar Mass of C = 12.0 u

Molar Mass of O = 16.0 u

Molar Mass of CaCO₃ = 40.1 + 12.0 + (16.0)(3) = 100 amu

(488 mol CaCO₃)(100 g CaCO₃) / (1 mol CaCO₃)

24. 10. Find the sum of the first five terms of the geometric sequence whose first termis 8 and the common ratio is -3.A. 243B. 488C. -243D. -488​


Answer:

B. 488

Step-by-step explanation:

Use the formula [tex]S_n = \frac{a(r^n-1)}{r-1}[/tex]

Given,

a = 8r = -3n = 5

Substitute the given value of a, r and n

[tex]S_5 = \frac{8(-3^5-1)}{-3-1}[/tex]

[tex]S_5 = \frac{8(-244)}{-4}[/tex]

[tex]S_5 = \frac{-1952}{-4}[/tex]

[tex]S_5 = 488[/tex]

∴ The sum of the first five terms of the geometric sequence is 488.
