4. portmanteau of twist and fiddle


A portmanteau is a word that is derived from blending two words with different sounds and meanings.

In the case of twist and fiddle, the product is “Twiddle”.

Twist means to curl or distort the shape, while fiddle means to fidget with something in a restless way. Since twiddle has combined the two, now it means to twist or fiddle something in a nervous manner.

18. 2.Learning Task 3. Given the square figure at the right, find the:1. Area of Fig. 12. Area of Fig. 23. Area of Fig. 34. Area of Fig. 45. Area of the whole figureFig.1Fig. 2Fig. 3Fig 4/2​


Areas:

1. x²

2. 2x

3. 2x

4. 4

5. x² + 4x + 4

Solutions:

1. Given: x - side of the square

Area of the Square = side x side

Find the area.

A = s x s

A = (x)(x)

A = x²

Therefore, the side of Figure 1 which is a square is x².

2. Given: 2 - width

x - length

Area of the Rectangle = length x width

Find the area.

A = length x width

A = (x)(2)

A = 2x

Therefore, the area of Figure 2 which is a rectangle is 2x.

3. Given: 2 - width

x - length

Area of the Rectangle = length x width

Find the area.

A = length x width

A = (x)(2)

A = 2x

Therefore, the area of Figure 3 which is a rectangle is 2x.

4. Given: 2 - side of the square

Area of the Square = side x side

Find the area.

A = 2 x 2

A = (2)(2)

A = 4

Therefore, the area of Figure 4 which is a square is 4.

5. Given: x + 2 - side of the whole figure

Area of the Square = side x side

Find the area.

A = side x side

A = (x + 2)(x + 2)

A = (x)(x) + (x)(2) + (2)(x) + (2)(2)

A = x² + 2x + 2x + 4

A = x² + 4x + 4

Therefore, the area of the whole figure is x² + 4x +4.
