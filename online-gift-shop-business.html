5. give me 5 issue on how online shopping affects local business​


Answer:

Here are 5 issues on how online shopping affects local businesses:

Decreased foot traffic: Online shopping has the potential to reduce the number of customers who visit physical stores.

Increased competition: Online shopping allows consumers to easily compare prices and products from a range of retailers, including those located outside of the local area.

Shipping costs: Online retailers often offer free shipping or low-cost shipping options, which can make it difficult for local businesses to compete.

Impact on employment: Local businesses are often significant employers in their communities.

Taxation issues: Online shopping can create challenges for local governments in terms of taxation. Online retailers may not be required to collect or remit local taxes, which can result in a loss of revenue for local governments.
