☞ 18, 75, 76, 79, 80, 80, 81, 83, 90, 94

☞ (18+75+76+79+80+80+81+83+90+94)/2

☞ 756/2

☞ 378

☞ 18, 75, 76, 79, 80, 80, 81, 83, 90, 94

☞ (80+80)/2

☞ 160/2

☞ 80

ModeMode is the most frequent value. The numbers may be double, triple, or quadruple.

☞ 18, 75, 76, 79, 80, 80, 81, 83, 90, 94
