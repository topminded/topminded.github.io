28. From your house, you drove 5km north and then stop at a nearby gas station for refueling. You then travel 3km in the same direction until you realized that you forget to get your change from the cashier. What is your total distance traveled and displacement?


Answer:

Total distance travelled: 11 km

Displacement: 5 km

Explanation:

The total distance travelled is 5 (north) then 3 (north again) and the 3 (back to the cashier)

So 5+3+3=11

Displacement however, is the measurement of change from your original point to your final destination.

Because your final destination is the cashier (which is only 5 km from your house), the displacement is also 5 km.

NOTE: Displacement has no relevance to the total distance travelled.
