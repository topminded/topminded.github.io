PROBLEM

In a Holcim Cement Plant, they produces quality concrete cements. The bulk cement powder truck contains 650.50 kilograms of cement powder. If the cement powder put in a 50-kilogram container, how many containers can be filled with cement powder?

SOLUTION

650.50 kilograms of cement powder × 50 kilogram container.

[tex]650.50 \times 50 = 32525[/tex]

Therefore there are 32,525 containers can be filled with cement powder.
