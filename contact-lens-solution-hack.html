21. LENS EQUATION SAMPLE PROBLEM WITH SOLUTIONS


Answer:

Problem:

A beam of light is incident on a convex lens with a focal length of 15 cm. If the object is placed 30 cm away from the lens, what is the image distance?

Solution:

The equation we will use to solve this problem is the Lens Equation:

1/f = 1/do + 1/di

where f = focal length of the lens, do = object distance, and di = image distance.

Substituting the given information into the equation, we have:

1/15 = 1/30 + 1/di

Solving for di, we get:

di = -20 cm

Therefore, the image distance is 20 cm away from the lens.
