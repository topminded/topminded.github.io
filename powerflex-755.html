2. 150 is 755 of what number?​

Step-by-step explanation:

150 is 755 percent of 19.8675. Explanation: What does 755 percent or 755% mean?

Percent (%) is an abbreviation for the Latin “per centum”, which means per hundred or for every hundred. So, 755% means 755 out of every 100.

Method 1: Diagonal multiplication to calculate "150 is 755 percent of what number".

As given: For 755, our answer will be 100

Assume: For 150, our answer will be X

755*X = 100*150 (In Steps 1 and 2 see colored text; Diagonal multiplications will always be equal)

X = 100*150/755 = 15000/755 = 19.8675

150 is 755 percent of 19.8675

Method 2: Same side division to calculate "150 is 755 percent of what number".

As given: For 755, our answer will be 100

Assume: For 150, our answer will be X

100/x = 755/150 (In Step 1 and 2, see colored text; Same side divisions will always be equal)

100/X = 755/150 or x/100 = 150/755

X = 150*100/150 = 15000/755 = 19.8675

150 is 755 percent of 19.8675
