1. 2. The most widely used universal color system used by designers a printers is the Pantone Color Matching System ​


Answer:

The Pantone Solid Colors palette is the most widely color palette produced by Pantone. The palette has a little more than 1100 unique colors and is primarily used in the print, graphics, and publishing industries. This is the palette that you are likely to find on most color printers.
