24. what is the value of 142 in binary digit?​


Answer:

10001110

Step-by-step explanation:

Step by step solution

Step 1: Divide (142)10 successively by 2 until the quotient is 0:

142/2 = 71, remainder is 0

71/2 = 35, remainder is 1

35/2 = 17, remainder is 1

17/2 = 8, remainder is 1

8/2 = 4, remainder is 0

4/2 = 2, remainder is 0

2/2 = 1, remainder is 0

1/2 = 0, remainder is 1

Step 2: Read from the bottom (MSB) to top (LSB) as 10001110.

So, 10001110 is the binary equivalent of decimal number 142 (Answer).
