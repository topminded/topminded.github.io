Answer:

1.Kissa-A love song of Datus and Princesses of the Tausug tribe.

2.Estijiro-Mindanao's ancient folk song and dance.

3.Miminsad-A ritual dance song and chant of Mindanao.

4.Sua Sua-A dance from Sulu which means "small orange plant".

5.Kambiok-A free rhythmic song of the Maranaos.
