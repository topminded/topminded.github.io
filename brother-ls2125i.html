20. i have a 3 brothers and each brother has 3 brother and how many are we? logic style


The answer will be FOUR.

Given :

He has a three brothers

Each brother has three brother

From the given itself, there are three brothers. So if he or she will include himself or herself, there will be four of them.

3+1=4

The self of the speaker was added to the number of his or her brothers.

The phrase "each brother has three brother" will not have any bearing since they were just pertaining to the number of their other numbers excluding theirselves.

4-1=3

Minus 1 since they have to subtract themselves.

Subtracting one, they have three brothers.
