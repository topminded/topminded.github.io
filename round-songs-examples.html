2.monophony, polyphony, and homophony.

- Monophony is the most basic of musical textures, consisting of a melody sung or played by a single instrument player without any accompanying harmony or chords.

-A texture in which a core part is supplemented by one or more other strands that fill out the harmony is known as homophony.
