16. meaningabout giving instructions?Purpose about giving instructions?Use about giving instructions?Example about giving instructions?​


Answer:

Giving instructions refers to providing directions, guidance, or information to help someone carry out a specific task or achieve a particular goal.

The purpose of giving instructions is to provide clarity and guidance to the recipient, enabling them to understand what is required of them and how to complete a task efficiently and effectively.

Instructions can be used in various contexts, including workplaces, educational settings, and everyday life. They can be verbal, written, or presented through visual aids, such as diagrams or videos.

An example of giving instructions could be a teacher providing step-by-step guidance to students on how to complete a science experiment or a manager instructing employees on how to use a new software program.
