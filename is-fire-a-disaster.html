3. Why fire is a natural disaster​


Answer:

The Environmental Protection Agency classifies wildfires as natural catastrophes. In nature, however, barely 10-15% of them occur on their own. Humans are responsible for the majority of wildfires, with unattended camp and trash fires, dropped cigarettes, and arson being the most prevalent causes <3
