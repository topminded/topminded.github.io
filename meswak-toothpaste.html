8. toothpaste ingredients​


Ingredients of Toothpaste:

1. Flouride

  • this is the mineral key to fight tooth decay. it also helps to make your

     teeth stronger and more resistant to acid water.

2. Glycerol

    • this ingredient keeps the toothpaste from drying out, it gives consistent

   • sorbitol plays to parts: along with glycerol, it helps hold the toothpaste

    • this common abrasive helps remove plaque, debris and surface stains.

    • this ingredient is a detergent, creating bubbles foam like soap and

       shampoo. sodium lauryl sulfate can cause mouth irritation and canker

       soars.

29. If 3 out of 5 dentist recommend white toothpaste, what percent do not recommend white toothpaste?


Representation:
    Let 5/5 represents the total number of dentists surveyed
          3/5 represents the dentists who recommend white toothpaste
 
Solution:  
     
     [tex] \frac{5}{5} - \frac{3}{5} = \frac{2}{5} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{2}{5} [/tex] is the fractional part of the dentists who do not recommend white tootpaste.

Convert fraction to percent:
     [tex] \frac{2}{5} [/tex] = 0.4   or  40%

ANSWER:  40% do not recommend white toothpaste.

If 3 out of 5 dentists recommend white tooth-paste, then 2 dentists do not recommend white toothpaste.

So, if 5 is increased to 100 (i.e. 5*20), 2 will be increased to 2*20 = 40

So, if 100 dentists are there, 40 dentists do not recommend white toothpaste.

Thus, 40% dentists do not recommend white-toothpaste.
