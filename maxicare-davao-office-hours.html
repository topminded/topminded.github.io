29. It takes 46 hours to make a land trip to davao at 35 kilometers per hour. how long should it take to make the same trip at the speed of 42 km per hour?


Answer:

It takes 38.33 hours for the same trip at 42 km/hour.

Step-by-step explanation:

The land trips have the same distance but with different speed rate and time.

Main formula:

Distance = Rate × Time

Given:

Rate₁ = 35 km per hour

Time₁ = 46 hours

Rate₂ = 42 km per hour

Time₂ = Unknown

Equation

Distance = Rate × Time

Since the distance are equal:

Distance₁ = Distance₂Rate₁ × Time₁ = Rate₂ × Time₂

35 km/hour × 46 hours = 42 km/hour × Time₂

1,610 km = 42 km/hour × Time₂

Time₂ = 1610 km × 1/42(hour/km)

Time₂ = 38.33 hours

It takes 38.33 hours for the same trip at 42 km/hour.

The distance traveled for each speed rate is 1,610 km.
