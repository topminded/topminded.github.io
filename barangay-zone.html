5. The danger zoned is declared to be within a radius of 5 km from the site of explosion. Suppose the site of explosion is at the origin. Is a certain barangay located at a place with coordinates (2,4) within the danger zone? Justify your answer. ​


Answer:

Yes

Step-by-step explanation:

Standard Equation of circle

[tex] {(x-h)} ^{2}+{(y-k)}^{2}={r}^{2}[/tex]

(h, k) = center, r = radius

[tex] \:[/tex]

With (0, 0) as origin and r = 5,

[tex] {(x-0)} ^{2}+{(y-0)}^{2}={5}^{2}[/tex]

[tex]{x}^{2}+{y}^{2}=25[/tex]

Clearly it resembles the Pythagorean triple (3,4,5)

Then,

(x, y) = (0, 5), (1, 2√6), (2,√21), (3, 4) vice versa

To know whether (2, 4) is part of the danger zone or not, we must know the range for the values of x and y

x = (0, 1, 2, 3, 2√6, 4,√21, 5)

y = (0, 1, 2, 3, 2√6, 4,√21, 5)

We can see the coordinate (2, 4) is in range of x and y

Therefore it is in the danger zone
