The pandemic was one of the most disastrous events that happened in the history of humankind. Despite its description, the pandemic still left us a valuable lesson that we can carry wherever we go.

Attached below is an example of a poster that you can recreate to represent the lessons or learnings we got from the pandemic. Here is the explanation:

Social distancing
The pandemic taught us how to be socially aware of our surroundings. This means social distancing should be observed at all times to reduce the risk of infection.

Healthy living
Since no one is excused for getting infected, we all need to take care of our bodies by drinking plenty of fluids and eating a well-balanced diet.

Quality time
Because of the pandemic, we got a chance to have quality time with our family because no one was allowed to go outside. It is a great lesson for all of us because we get a chance to bond with our family.
