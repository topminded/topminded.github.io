17. Characteristics of laundry detergent​


Answer:

Detergents are commonly available as powders or concentrated solutions. Detergents, like soaps, work because they are amphiphilic: partly hydrophilic (polar) and partly hydrophobic (non-polar). Their dual nature facilitates the mixture of hydrophobic compounds (like oil and grease) with water.

19. Characteristics of laundry detergent​


Characteristics of Laundry Detergent:A laundry detergent should be soapy.It can either have a fresh soapy smell or it can be fragrant free.It should form bubbles while it is being used in the water.It can either be powdered or liquid.It should be able to clean clothes.What is a laundry Detergent?

Laundry detergents are used to clean clothes. When we put it in water, it should form bubbles. It helps to clean our clothes faster. There are two types of laundry detergent. These are Powder Detergent and Liquid Detergent.

What is a Powder Detergent?

Powder detergent is a laundry detergent that is made of powder. We put in in the water and let it dissolve before we use it. When it is already dissolved in the water, we can already use it to wash our clothes.

What is Liquid Detergent?

Liquid detergent is a laundry detergent that is in liquid form. We just need to pour it in water and we can use it to wash our clothes.

Importance of Laundry Detergents:It helps us clean our clothes faster that using water only.It kills the viruses and germs in our clothes.It can also be used to clean surfaces of our bathrooms.Safety Tips in Using Laundry Detergents:If you are a minor using detergents, always ask an adult to assist you.Never leave a child alone with detergents.Wash your hands thoroughly after using these so that it won't spread to the other things you touch.
