Powerful Women In The Philippines

oleh flex05

Although the Philippines appears to be ahead of its neighboring countries by having two women presidents already, major hurdles are still in place for women to gain a firmer foothold in politics. It is still basically an elite-male dominated political landscape. While there is a perceptible increase in the number of women elected into government posts, assumption to institutional leadership does not necessarily translate into the expected degree of political clout. The question of resources also affects women's access to decision-making positions since women and men do not enjoy the same access to resources that yields power. The lack of economic power, similarly, deprives women of their rights and opportunities. Mylene Hega. (2003 March) Participation of Women in Philippine Politics and Society: A Situationer. 3.
