27. University of Mindanao logo meaning of Vinta Sail,Laurel leaves,Five Star,Blue Color & "Vincit Omnia Veritas" ? anyone know the meaning?


The inner circle has the Latin words, “Vincit Omnia Veritas,”meaning Truth Conquers All.


Over it is two (2) laurel leaf branches symbolizing honor and achievement, framing a vinta on a wavy sea, whose giant sail has the initials of UM, with the horizon behind. This conveys the message “Sail On” or “Move On.”In the sky above the horizon are five (5) stars representing the heavenly bodies that influence the destiny of the youth, Hope of the Fatherland.
