7. 4. A group of 6 friends is thinking about a summer break trip to Palawan. At least 4 of them should make an early booking to avail of the group discount flight. How many groups can be formed such that they can avail of the discount?,​


Answer:

360

Step-by-step explanation:

P ( n, r ) = n! ÷ (n-r)!

P ( 6, 4 ) = 6! ÷ (6-4)!

= 6! ÷ 2!

= 720 ÷ 2

= 360

360 groups can be formed

11. A packages products to form a trip for an overseas market travelling to your country.​


Sure, I can provide you with some ideas for packaging products to form a trip for an overseas market travelling to your country. The specific products and destinations will depend on the country you are targeting and the interests of your target market, but here are some general tips to get you started:

1. Consider the main attractions: Think about the top tourist attractions in your country and how you can package them together into a cohesive trip. For example, if your country has famous historical landmarks or museums, you could create a cultural tour that includes visits to these sites.

2. Mix and match experiences: Try to create a diverse range of experiences within your trip package. This could include a combination of outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, and relaxation time. Consider including activities like hiking, kayaking, or visiting a spa to appeal to a wide range of travelers.

3. Provide local cuisine: Food is often a big part of the travel experience, so make sure to include local cuisine in your trip package. Consider offering a food tour or cooking class to give travelers a taste of the local flavors.

4. Offer flexibility: Not all travelers have the same preferences or schedules, so it's important to offer some flexibility within your trip package. This could mean offering optional activities or excursions, or allowing travelers to customize their itinerary.

5. Highlight unique features: Finally, make sure to highlight any unique features or experiences that are specific to your country. This could be anything from a local festival to a hidden gem attraction that many travelers might not know about. By showcasing these unique features, you'll set your trip package apart from others and give travelers a one-of-a-kind experience.
