FURTHER EXPLANATION

You can use a proportional symbol, a direct or inverse proportion, or both to represent the relationship between two quantities.

Signs of Direct and Inverse Proportion

X ∝ Y  --- This is how directly proportional symbol denotesX ∝ 1/Y  --- This is how inversely proportional symbol denotes

We use the terms "X is Directly Proportional to Y" or "Y is Directly Proportional to X" to describe two quantities when they are directly proportional to one another. We use the terms "X is Inversely Proportional to Y" or "Y is Inversely Proportional to X" to describe two quantities when they are inversely proportional to one another.
