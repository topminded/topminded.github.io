18. Aiden and Daniel have been good friends for many years. The age of Aiden is 5 9 of Daniel's age. 6 years later, the ratio of Aiden's age to Daniel's age will be 13 : 21. How old is Aiden now?


Answer:

Aiden is now 18 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: The current ratio of Aiden's age to Daniel's age is 5:9.

Step 2: 6 years later, the ratio will be 13:21.

Step 3: Solve for the new age by using the ratio: Aiden's new age = (13/21) x 9 = 5.5 x 9 = 49.5.

Step 4: Subtract 6 years from Aiden's new age to get his current age: 49.5 - 6 = 43.5.

Step 5: Since Aiden's age is in years, round up to the nearest whole number: 43.5 rounded up is 44.

Step 6: Therefore, Aiden is now 18 years old.
