1. What is the importance of ramping plan?​


Answer:

When "ramp up" comes up in press releases or on conference calls, it typically signals management confidence in the future of the business; however, the prudent investor should be on the watch for too much exuberance.

Explanation:

Ramp up is a significant increase in the level of output of a company's products or services. A ramp-up typically occurs in anticipation of an imminent increase in demand. While it is generally a feature of smaller companies at an early stage of development, a ramp up can also be undertaken by large companies that are rolling out new products or expanding in new geographies.

12. Kendrick holds the rubber blue ball halfway up the ramp and releases ir (figure 1).The ball rolls down the ramp and along the floor beyond the end of the ramp. What force caused the ball to roll down the ramp?


Answer:

The force that caused the ball to roll down the ramp is gravity. Gravity is the force that pulls objects towards each other, and in this case, it is the force that pulls the ball towards the center of the Earth. As the ball is released from its position halfway up the ramp, gravity causes it to accelerate downward, rolling down the ramp and along the floor beyond the end of the ramp. The steeper the ramp, the greater the force of gravity and the faster the ball will roll down the ramp.
