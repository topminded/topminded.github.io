Commercial Sand and Gravel Permit

Any qualified person may be granted a permit by the provincial governor to extract and remove sand and gravel or other loose or unconsolidated materials which are used in their natural state, without undergoing processing from an area of not more than five hectares (5 has.)

An Exploration Permit (EP) is an initial mode of entry in mineral exploration allowing a Qualified Person to undertake exploration activities for mineral resources in certain areas open to mining in the country

Quarry Permits are issued by the Inspector of Mines and expire on 31st December of any given year for the extraction of material below 16,000 cu. yards. There are Quarry Permits (QP), Registered Quarry Permits (RQP), Registered Artisanal Quarry Permits (RAQP), and Non-Commercial Quarry Permits (NCQP).
