29. Write down the 5 PILLARS OF CRIMINAL JUSTICE SYSTEM OF THE PHILIPPINES. And write there duties and function.​


Explanation:

The five pillars of the criminal justice system of the Philippines are as follows:

1. Law Enforcement - This pillar is responsible for enforcing the law and maintaining peace and order in the community. It includes the Philippine National Police (PNP), the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI), and other law enforcement agencies. Their duties and functions include investigating crimes, arresting suspects, and gathering evidence to build cases against accused individuals.

2. Prosecution - This pillar is responsible for prosecuting criminal cases in court. It includes the Office of the Prosecutor and the Department of Justice (DOJ). Their duties and functions include reviewing the evidence gathered by law enforcement agencies, preparing cases for trial, and presenting evidence in court.

3. Judiciary - This pillar is responsible for adjudicating criminal cases and ensuring that justice is served. It includes the Supreme Court, the Court of Appeals, and the trial courts. Their duties and functions include hearing cases, evaluating evidence, and rendering judgments.

4. Correctional - This pillar is responsible for rehabilitating offenders and ensuring that they are reintegrated into society. It includes the Bureau of Corrections (BuCor), the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology (BJMP), and other correctional institutions. Their duties and functions include providing education and vocational training to inmates, as well as counseling and other forms of rehabilitation.

5. Community - This pillar is responsible for promoting community involvement in the criminal justice system. It includes non-governmental organizations (NGOs), community-based organizations (CBOs), and other community groups. Their duties and functions include raising awareness about crime prevention, promoting restorative justice, and advocating for the rights of victims and offenders.


30. Ano ang paglilingkod ng Department of Justice


Answer:

Ang Kagawaran ng Katarungan, o Department of Justice (DOJ) ay departamento sa ilalim ng Ehekutibong Sangay ng Pamahalaang Pilipinas na responsable sa pagtataguyod ng panuntunan ng batas sa Pilipinas. Ito ang pangunahing ahensiya ng gobyerno na nagsisilbi sa pamamagitan ng pagbibigay ng legal na payo, at ang pangunahing ahensiya at braso sa pag-uusig. Ang Kagawaran ng Katarungan ay institusyon na nagsisilbi bilang pangunahing taga-usig ng pamahalaan at pinangangasiwaan ang criminal system ng hustisya ng gobyerno sa pamamagitan ng pagsisiyasat sa mga krimen, pag-uusig sa mga nagkasala, at pangangasiwa sa sistema ng pagwawasto.
