Ingredients

3 pounds bone-in pork chops, about 1/4 inch thick

1/2 cup calamansi or lemon juice

1/4 cup soy sauce

1 large onion, peeled and sliced thinly

1 head garlic, peeled and minced

1/4 teaspoon pepper

3 tablespoons canola oil

1 cup water

salt to taste

1 small onion, peeled and sliced into rings

Explanation:

The ingredients for pork steak can vary depending on the recipe and personal preference, but here is a basic list of ingredients for a simple and tasty pork steak:

Pork steaks (cut from the shoulder or loin)SaltBlack pepperGarlic powder or minced garlicOlive oil or vegetable oil

Optional ingredients:

PaprikaCayenne pepperOnion powderWorcestershire sauceSoy sauceLemon juiceBrown sugar

You can season the pork steaks with a mixture of salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and any other optional ingredients you prefer. Then, heat up a skillet with oil and cook the pork steaks for several minutes on each side until they are browned and cooked through.


3. write your own procedure for the given "basic pork steak" recipe.answer this pls..'brainliest ^ ^​


Answer:

Ingredients

4 pieces pork chops

5 tablespoons soy sauce

2 pieces lime or calamansi

1/2 cup cooking oil

2 pieces onions sliced

1 1/2 cups water

1/2 teaspoon granulated white sugar

Salt and pepper to taste

Explanation:

Instructions

1. Combine pork chops, soy sauce, and lime juice in a large bowl or resealable bag. Marinate for at least 1 hour.

2. Heat a pan then pour-in cooking oil.

3. Pan-fry the marinated pork chops in medium heat for 3 minutes per side.

4. Remove excess oil. Pour-in the remaining marinade and water and let boil. Simmer for 45 minutes or until the pork is tender. Note: Add water as needed.

5. Add-in the sugar, salt, and pepper then stir.

6. Put-in the onions and cook for 3 minutes more.

7. Turn-off heat and transfer to a serving plate.

8. Serve. Share and enjoy!

23. procedures in making pork embutido and the engredients ​


Answer:

ingredients

• 1 lbs. Ground Pork

• 3 pcs. Hotdog, cut in half

• 2 pcs. Eggs, hard-boiled

• ¼ cup Sweet Pickle Relish

• ¼ cup Tomato Sauce

• 1 Egg, raw

• 1 cup Cheddar Cheese

• 1/2 cup Red Bell Pepper, minced

• 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper, minced

• ¾ cup Raisins

• ½ cup Carrots, minced

• ½ cup Onion, minced

• Salt and Pepper

• 1 cup Bread Crumbs

Instructions (procedure)

1. In a Bowl, Mix all ingredient well. (Except Hotdogs and Boiled Eggs)

2. Spread the mixture on an aluminum foil.

3. Arrange the Hotdogs and hard boiled eggs on top of the mixtures.

4. Roll together with the foil into a cylindrical shape.

5. Steam for an hour.

6. Chill in the refrigerator.

7. Slice and Serve.
