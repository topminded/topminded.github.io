[tex] \large \bold \color{blue}{ANSWER}[/tex]

What are the different kinds of logo?

》The different kinds of logos can be placed into seven categories: emblems, pictorial marks, logotypes, lettermarks, abstract logos, mascot logos, and combination logos.

What is personal logo?

》Personal logos are the artist's own brand. An artist's personal logo are an extension of the artist's ethos or character.

Where do personal logos used?

》Personal logos and branding are used by creative professionals to add another dimension of identity to their work.

What is crest logo?

》Crest logos are rooted in deep symbolism. Often designed after the elaborate crests made in medieval Europe, their basic makeup usually involves a shield, scroll, or two mirrored animals.

How does a logo design process go?

》Develop ideas & decide on art direction. Create logo concepts based on strategy.
