The main steps in planning a project are:Scope planning is the process of defining the project's needs so that the work breakdown structure can be made.Preparing the work breakdown structure, which means writing down how the project will be broken down into tasks and subtasks.Project schedule development means making a list of all the activities and showing how they will be done.Resource planning is figuring out who will do what work, when and if they need any special skills to finish the project tasks.Planning a budget means figuring out how much it will cost to finish the project.Planning for buying things means paying attention to vendors outside of your company and using subcontractors.Risk management is planning for possible risks and thinking about backup plans and ways to reduce the impact of those risks.Planning for quality means looking at the quality criteria that will be used for the project.Communication planning is making a plan for how everyone involved in a project will talk to each other.
