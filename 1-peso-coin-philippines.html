5 pesos coin: 11.5 grams

1 million 1 peso coins: 6.1 metric tons (6,100 kilograms)

1 million 5 pesos coins: 11.5 metric tons (11,500 kilograms)

1 million 10 pesos coins: 8.5 metric tons (8,500 kilograms)

1 million 25 pesos coins: 7.7 metric tons (7,700 kilograms)

2. 1. The Philippine peso coin has a mass of 5.5 grams and consists of 75% copper and 25% nickel. What mass of copper and nickel are present in a one-peso coin?​


Answer:

Total mass: 5.5 grams

Total Copper (75%): 4.125 grams

Total Nickel (25%): 1.375 grams

Explanation:

• Multiply the Total mass to the percentage of the mass of copper and nickel. (P.S. Convert Percentage to Decimals)

5.5g (Total mass) × 0.75 (percentage of Cu) = 4.125g

5.5g (Total mass) × 0.25 (percentage of Ni) =

1.375g

• Add the mass of Copper and Nickel to confirm the total mass.

4.125g (Cu) + 1.375 (Ni) = 5.5 grams
