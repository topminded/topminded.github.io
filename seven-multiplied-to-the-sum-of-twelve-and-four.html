[tex]\rm\large{Good \: Morning!}[/tex]

DIRECTIONS:

Tell whether the following is expression or equation.

A. Expression

B. Equation

ANSWERS:

1. A number increased by four is twelve.

n + 4 = 12It is an Equation. Why? Because it contains a variable, number, operation symbol, and an equal sign.

FINAL ANSWER:

Therefore, the answer is B. Equation.

2. Thirty is equal to twice a number decreased by four.

30 = 2n - 4It is an Equation. Still, it consists of variable, number, Operation symbol, and an equal sign.

FINAL ANSWER:

Therefore, the answer is B. Equation.

3. Thirty taken from five times K.

5K - 30It is an Expression. Why? Because it contains a variable, number, operation symbol, but still it doesn't have an equal sign.

FINAL ANSWER:

Therefore, the answer is A. Expression.

4. Five times a number is fifty.

5a = 50It is an Equation. It has an equal sign.

FINAL ANSWER:

Therefore, the answer is B. Equation.

5. Seven multiplied by the sum of twelve and four.

7(12 + 4)It is an Expression. Why? It only contains operation symbol and numbers, but doesn't have an equal sign.

==============================================

- LEARN MORE -

Expressions are mathematical terms usually consisting of numbers, operation symbols, and variables.

Example: 2a + 7

On the other hand, Equations, are group of mathematical terms, and usually contains numbers, variables, operation symbols, and an equal sign.

Example: 3x - 9 = 8

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE?

To be able to easily determine which is equation and which is expression, we have a clue. Always remember, that Equations always contain an equal sign, while Expressions doesn't have.
