11. Activity 2. Instruction: Fill in the blanks, choose your answer inside the box below.consumption, inedible, crowded, palate, eggs, plate, garnishes, servers, sizes, ill1. Textures and flavors hit____ the at different times.2. If your dessert is beautiful, it shouldn't need a lot of____.3. Choose garnishes that are the correct_____; they should be easy to eat.4. Desserts containing uncooked _____ should be handled with extreme care.5. Keep away from food when you are______.6. When plating desserts, the choice of _________ is critical to the final presentation.7. Keep the rim of the plate clear, so the _____ aren't touching the food when they place the desserts in front of the customers.8. Never decorate a plate with something _____.9. Choosing the right size of plate is important because food should not be _____ onto the plate.10. Any dessert that is not required for immediate ______must be cooled rapidly and stored in the cool room untilrequired.plsss help​


Answer

1. Textures and flavors hit the PALATE at different times.

Explanation: The palate used here comes from the British definition, a sense of taste

2. If your dessert is beautiful, it shouldn't need a lot of GARNISHES.

Explanation: Food shouldn't be over-decorated with garnish because the flavor is the most important aspect of a dish.

3. Choose garnishes that are the correct SIZES; they should be easy to eat.

Explanation: Garnishes are for decoration more than consumption. Its purpose is to elevate the main ingredient of a dish.

4. Desserts containing uncooked EGGS should be handled with extreme care.

Explanation: Raw eggs contain a germ called salmonella which can make you seek.

5. Keep away from food when you are ILL.

Explanation: Another way to put this statement is, "Don't cook when you're ill"

6. When plating desserts, the choice of PLATE is critical to the final presentation.

Explanation: The plate is like the frame of a painting. In this application, the dessert is the subject.

7. Keep the rim of the plate clear, so the SERVERS aren't touching the food when they place the desserts in front of the customers.

Explanation: For health and sanitation reasons, a server's hand shouldn't touch the food to avoid possible contamination.

8. Never decorate a plate with something INEDIBLE.

Explanation: Inedible decorations are a hazard to people

9. Choosing the right size of plate is important because food should not be CROWDED onto the plate.

Explanation: Crowding a plate causes the flavors to mix and change.

10. Any dessert that is not required for immediate CONSUMPTION must be cooled rapidly and stored in the cool room until

required.

Explanation: You'll often read the phrase "for immediate consumption" when buying food. It means to eat it soon or the food will spoil.

30. There are 5 girls in one roomAnne is cookingMarie is playing chessBrenda is reading a bookJoy is preparing the plateswhat was the 5th girl do?​


Answer:Three possibilities

The obvious one is to answer the fifth sister is playing chess with Kate. However, playing chess does not mean playing a game of chess. If you are tinkering with the pieces with no opponent, you are playing chess, but not necessarily with someone as in a game.

If not playing a game of chess with Kate, what’s the fifth sister doing? So we’re back to the same question. Well, the fifth sister was probably writing this entry into her journal telling us what sisters were doing at this time. Thus, we know there are five sisters busy with their activities.

However, you might pause and think what kind of room has laundry, TV, reading and playing areas, plus kitchen to cook. A studio apartment perhaps, but not a room. One explanation is a movie set. The fifth sister is the movie director shooting a movie about her siblings.
