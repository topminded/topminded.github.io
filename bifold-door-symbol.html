22. The ratio of doors to windows is 1:5. There are 186 doors and windows in a building. How many doors are there?


The ratio of doors to windows is 1:5. There are 186 doors and windows in a building. How many doors are there?

Solution:

1 + 5 = 6 parts

There are 6 parts of 186

186 ÷ 6 = 31

31 is 1 part of 186

Doors: 31 × 1 = 31

Windows: 31 × 5 = 155

Let's check if our answer was right!

31 + 155 = 186Final Answer:There are 31 doors and 155 windows in the building.

Romeo rides on a bus. Near the door he sees a symbol. The reading style is Intensive Reading.

What is Intensive Reading?

Intensive Reading is used for shorter texts to get the main information or idea. Since Romeo only needs to see a symbol, he will immediately get the idea of the symbol or short text included in it.

Skimming is a reading style of reading a piece of text to know its main idea. This is only looking for the main information and not thoroughly reading on the whole passage.
