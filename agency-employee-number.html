10. A call center agency has two salary plans for it's employees under a plan A An employee receives PhP300.00 plus PhP30.00 for each additional working hour under plan B An employee receives Php400.00 plus PhP20.00 for each additional working hour what number of additional hours would result in the same salary amount​


Let X be the number of additional working hours.

Under plan A, the salary is 300 + 30X.

Under plan B, the salary is 400 + 20X.

We want to find the X that makes the two salaries equal:

300 + 30X = 400 + 20X

10X = 100

X = 10

Therefore, working 10 additional hours would result in the same salary amount under both plans.

22. a certain employment agency has 20 employees.Skilled employed are paid Php 400.00 a day and unskilled employees are paid Php 270.00 a day.of the total wages amount Php 6,570.00 per day,how many skilled employees are there?


Answer:

There are 9 skilled and 11 unskilled employees.

Step-by-step explanation:

Skilled employees: x

Unskilled employees: y

Total employees: 20

Equation (1):

x + y = 20

y = 20-x  ⇒  unskilled employees in terms of x

Equation (2):

400x + 270(20-x) = 6,570

400x + 5,400 - 270x = 6,570

130x = 6,570 - 5,400

130x/130 = 1,170/130

x = 9

Substitute 9 to x:

Number of skill employees: x = 9

Number of unskilled employees: 20-9 = 11

There are 9 skilled employees and 11 unskilled employees.

Check:

Number of employees:

9 + 11 = 20

20 = 20 (True)

Wages:

400(9) + 270(11) = 6,570

3,600 + 2,970 = 6,570

6,570 = 6,570 (True)
