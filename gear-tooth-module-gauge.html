Answer:

1. The gear ratio is 4:1, which can be written as 4/1 or 4.

2. The smaller gear turns four times for every turn of the larger gear.

3. Possible sizes of two gears with a gear ratio of 3 to 1 include: a larger gear with 60 teeth and a smaller gear with 20 teeth; a larger gear with 90 teeth and a smaller gear with 30 teeth; and a larger gear with 120 teeth and a smaller gear with 40 teeth.
