A CVC word consists of a consonant, a vowel, and a consonant sound. When a vowel is followed by a consonant, the syllable closes and the vowel says its sound. 16. what is the molarity of aluminum ion ina solution that contains 1.25 mol of aluminum sulfate in 875 mL?​


Answer:

Molarity is 1.43 mol/L or 1.43 M

Explanation:

M = n/v

where: M = molarity

n = moles of solute

v = volume of solution

Given:

n = 1.25 mol

v = 0.875 L

Required:

Molarity

Solution:

M = n/v

M = 1.25/0.875

M = 1.43 mol/L

25. What is the percent mass of aluminum in a sample of ore that contains 3.85 g of aluminum and 3.50g of oxygen​


To find the percent mass of aluminum in the sample of ore, we need to determine the total mass of the sample first.

Total mass of the sample = mass of aluminum + mass of oxygen

Total mass of the sample = 3.85 g + 3.50 g

Total mass of the sample = 7.35 g

Now, we can calculate the percent mass of aluminum in the sample by dividing the mass of aluminum by the total mass of the sample and then multiplying by 100 to get a percentage:

Percent mass of aluminum = (mass of aluminum / total mass of the sample) x 100

Percent mass of aluminum = (3.85 g / 7.35 g) x 100

Percent mass of aluminum = 52.38%

Therefore, the percent mass of aluminum in the sample of ore is 52.38%.
