11. An item with a list price is offered at a discount of 32%. What is the net price?​


Try to multiply the given number to .32 and get the total... when you get the total amount try to minus the original price to the total (the one that you multiply).

Example: the given amount is 100php and get the 32%

100 x .32 = 32.00

So, the 100 (original price) and the 32.00 (the total)... try to minus each other and you will get 68php (which is the net price)
