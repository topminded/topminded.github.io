2. 1. How will advances in smartphones and apps impact the marketing, distribution and delivery of aviation products?


Answer:

Advances in smartphones and apps will have a significant impact on the marketing, distribution, and delivery of aviation products. Smartphones and apps provide a wide range of opportunities for airlines to reach out to potential customers in a more convenient and cost-effective way. Airlines can use mobile apps to create personalized marketing campaigns, offer discounts and promotions, and provide customers with more detailed information about their products. This will help airlines to better target and reach their customers, leading to increased sales. Additionally, apps can be used to streamline the booking process, allowing customers to book flights and other services quickly and easily. This will improve customer satisfaction and reduce the amount of time and resources needed to process orders. Finally, apps can be used to provide customers with real-time updates on their flights, allowing them to make more informed decisions when planning their journeys.

23. Identify and discuss the advantage and disadvantage between Desktop apps and Mobile apps


Answer &

Explanation:

The Advantages of Desktop Apps:

No annual or monthly fee. ...No reliance on an internet connection. ...Much easier to customize for your needs. ...You get all of the benefits of a desktop solution and can still access the software from a remote computer.

The Advantages of Mobile Apps:

Ease of access – for the user, clicking your app is far quicker and easier than accessing yours or a competitor’s website even if they’ve bookmarked it.

Data Capture – valuable data can be gathered from apps concerning use, activity and buying habits.

Communication – it’s easier to interact with your customers and prospects with an app including the immediacy of being able to attract their attention and maybe prompt action through, for example, push notifications.

Staff interaction – in business information can be passed quickly and easily to staff whether accessing an internal company portal wherever they are or for communicating with those working remotely.

Productivity – can be vastly increased through app based interactions with staff and outside contractors.

The Disadvantages of Desktop Apps:

Not PortableHard Drive SpaceMandatory InstallationManual UpdatesDeployment for Each PC

The Disadvantages of Mobile Apps:

Lack of clarityCostComplicated Updating
