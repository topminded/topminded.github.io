17. What will be your preparations if you are going to deliver a speech presentation in public?


Answer:

1.Your note cards (or cue sheets) must have enough information on them to be able to deliver the speech without missing details and organized

2.You should be able to glance at the cards, get your bearings, and look back at the audience. If you are reading the cards word-for-word, there are too many words on them, unless it is an extended exact quote, or group of statistics that must be delivered precisely.

3.Be sure your notes or cards are numbered (e.g., boldly in the upper right hand corner), so you can keep them organized.

4.Avoid writing or printing on two sides; flipping a page or card is distracting to the audience. The audience should not be aware of the notes.

5.Rehearse your speech using the notes that you will bring to the podium. Be sure you can glance at the notes, get your information, and look up to have eye contact with the audience.
