Answer:

Explanation:

During the pandemic, because of the absence of face-to-face classes,  learning has been more difficult. To face, most students really are not interested in studying. They only do so due to it being their only responsibility and the social benefits it provides. Example, this gives them a break from their home life, they interact with people, form groups, make friends, and create fun memories.

Without a social environment added with hefty loads of academic work, students feel tired and jaded. Worst, this causes mental health exhaustion. To address this problem, there should be a regular academic break. There must be forums on where students can address their problems. The teachers should also avoid giving impossible tasks.
