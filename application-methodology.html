12. SAQ-1: Which sampling methodology/technique/procedure do you think is best to use whenconducting a qualitative research; probability sampling, non-probability sampling or both can beapplicable?pa tulongg​


Answer:

ANSWER & EXPLANATION:

•When conducting a qualitative research, the sampling methodology/technique/procedure I think the best to use are "PURPOSIVE/JUDGMENTAL SAMPLING" and "CONVENIENCE SAMPLING" because they align the best across nearly all qualitative research designs. Sampling techniques can be used in conjunction with one another very easily or can be used alone within a qualitative dissertation.

•When conducting a probability sampling, the sampling methodology/technique/procedure I think the best to use are "SIMPLE RANDOM SAMPLING", "SYSTEMATIC SAMPLING", "STRATIFIED SAMPLING", and "CLUSTER SAMPLING".

•When conducting a non-probability sampling, the sampling methodology/technique/procedure I think the best to use is "CONVENIENCE SAMPLING" because it is where samples are selected from the population only because they are conveniently available to the researcher.
