13. Discuss the acceptability and commercial use of sex violence, and gambling in the Philippines?​


Answer:

These industries exist in the Philippines and are accepted by some sections of society.

In terms of sex violence, there are laws in the Philippines that prohibit any form of sexual violence, including rape and sexual harassment. Despite this, there are still reports of incidents of sexual violence in the country, and more efforts are needed to address this issue. The use of sex violence in media, such as films or TV series, can also be a controversial topic in the Philippines. While there are no explicit laws prohibiting the depiction of sexual violence in media, there have been calls for stricter regulations to prevent any glorification or normalization of such acts.

On the other hand, gambling is legal and widely accepted in the Philippines. The country has a number of casinos and lotteries, and many people enjoy gambling as a form of entertainment. However, the government has also implemented regulations to ensure that these industries are properly regulated and do not contribute to social ills such as addiction or crime rates.

Overall, while sex violence and gambling are present in the Philippines, the country is also taking steps to prevent any negative impacts on society and ensure that these industries are properly regulated.
