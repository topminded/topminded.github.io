15. A motorboat leaves a harbour and travels at an average speed of 8 km/h toward a small island. Two hours later, a cabin cruiser leaves the same harbour and travels at an average speed of 16 km/h toward the same island. How many hours after the cabin cruiser leaves will it be alongside the motorboat?


[tex] \large \mathcal{SOLUTION:} [/tex]

[tex] \!\!\begin{array}{l} \textsf{Let }t\textsf{ be the time in hours for the cabin cruiser to} \\ \textsf{overtake the motorboat after leaving the harbour.} \\ \\ \textsf{It is given that the cabin cruiser leaves the same} \\ \textsf{harbour 2 hours later.} \\ \\ \textsf{After }t\textsf{ hours, the distance of cabin cruiser and}\\ \textsf{motorboat from the harbour are equal.} \\ \\ \begin{array}{| c | c | c |} \hline & \bold{Speed} & \bold{Time} \\ \hline \textsf{Motorboat} & 8\textsf{ km/h} & (t + 2)\textsf{ hours} \\ \hline \textsf{Cabin Cruiser} & 16\textsf{ km/h} & t\textsf{ hours} \\ \hline \end{array} \\ \\ \textsf{We know that }\bold{Distance} = \bold{Speed} \times \bold{Time}. \\ \\ \textsf{Solving for }t, \\ \\ \begin{aligned} \qquad 8(t + 2) &= 16t \\ 8t + 16 &= 16t \\ 16t - 8t &= 16 \\ 8t &=16 \\ t &=\boxed{2\textsf{ hours}}\quad\textit{Answer} \end{aligned}\end{array} [/tex]

Find the number of residents who listens to radio only.a. 180b.390c.270d.1502.In a community, all the residents watch either Netflix or Disney Plus or both channels. 300 residents watch Netflix channel, 270 residents watch Disney Plus channel and 120 residents watch both channels. Find the total number of residents in the community.a.690b.450c.700d.570


Answer:

1. To find the number of residents who listen to radio only, we can use the formula:

(number of residents who listen to radio) - (number of residents who watch both TV and listen to radio) = number of residents who listen to radio only

So, we have:

270 - 120 = 150

Therefore, the answer is d. 150 residents listen to radio only.

2. To find the total number of residents in the community, we can use the formula:

(total number of residents) = (number of residents who watch Netflix) + (number of residents who watch Disney Plus) - (number of residents who watch both Netflix and Disney Plus)

So, we have:

(total number of residents) = 300 + 270 - 120 = 450

Therefore, the answer is b. 450 residents in the community.

28. The town of Brookfield sent out a survey to its residents to determine what new landmark they should build in the City Center. Of the residents who were surveyed 2/5, of the residents voted for a park, 1/4 of the residents voted for a garden, and the remaining residents voted for a zoo. If a total of 14,000 residents were surveyed, how many residents voted for a zoo?


The proportion of residents who voted for a park is 2/5, and the proportion who voted for a garden is 1/4. Therefore, the proportion of residents who voted for a zoo is:

1 - (2/5 + 1/4) = 1 - (8/20 + 5/20) = 1 - (13/20) = 7/20

If 7/20 of the residents voted for a zoo, and a total of 14,000 residents were surveyed, the number of residents who voted for a zoo is:

(7/20) x 14,000 = 4,900

Therefore, 4,900 residents voted for a zoo.
