1. describe potty chair​


A potty or potty chair is a proportionately small chair or enclosure with an opening for seating very young children to "go potty." It is a variant of the close stool which was used by adults before the widespread adoption of water flushed toilets. Potty chairs are used during potty training, a.k.a. toilet training.
