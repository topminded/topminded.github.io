I am writing to inform you that I was unable to attend [class/meeting/exam/etc.] on [date] due to an illness. I apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and I would like to request that I be allowed to make up any missed work or assignments.

I became ill [specific time frame, e.g. "over the weekend"], with [describe symptoms and severity, e.g. "a high fever and persistent cough"]. 26. Excuse Letter for Being absent in school due to Fever


Answer:

EXCUSE LETTER

Excuse letter is a formal written notification  asking for apology,  it is a note of explanation of an absence. Excuse letter is expected from a students who has unexpectedly missed a day in school.  Excuse letter should include time and the reason of absence. The letter is usually short and simple that contains basic facts.

Parts of an excuse letter

1. Heading - It includes the return address and date

2.Greetings

3. Body

4. Closing

Example of an excuse letter

May 7, 2019

123 Kamias Street, Paranaque

Metro Manila, Philippines

Dear Maam:

Good day!

Please excuse me for being absent in your class today, July 5, 2019 because I have a fever. I was not able to go to school because I feel so dizzy.  I hope that you will understand me regarding this matter and allow me to have a remedial activities on the lesson I had missed.

Thank you for your kind consideration.

Sincerely yours,

ANGEL VEM

Grade 3 - Ilang-Ilang
