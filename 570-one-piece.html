[ Commonwealth Act No. 570, June 07, 1940 ]

AN ACT MAKING THE FILIPINO NATIONAL LANGUAGE AN OFFICIAL LANGUAGE FROM THE FOURTH OF JULY, NINETEEN HUNDRED AND FORTY-SIX.

Be it enacted by the National Assembly of the Philippines:

SECTION 1. The Filipino national language is declared as one of the official languages of the Philippines, effective the fourth day of July, nineteen hundred and forty-six.

SEC. 2. For the purpose of this Act, all textbooks for the primary schools written in the national language or which are necessary for the propagation thereof, by the medium of public and private schools and other agencies and methods of popularization, shall be prepared under the supervision of the Bureau of Education, subject to the approval of the Institute of National Language in so far as its language form is connected.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 7, 1940.

25. Batas komonwelt blg. 570


Batas Komonwelt Blg. 570 o Commonwealth Act No. 570

Ang Batas Komonwelt Blg. 570 ay ang batas na nagtalaga sa Wikang Filipino bilang Opisyal na Wika ng Bansang Pilipinas. Ito ay naisabatas noong ika-7 ng Hunyo taong 1940 ni dating pangulo Manuel L. Quezon.

Ang Batas Komonwelt Blg. 570 ay isa sa mga batas na may malaking gampanin sa pagkatatag ng kasalukuyang wikang opisyal. Ang mga sumusunod ay ilan pang halimbawa ng mahahalagang batas tungkol sa wikang Filipino:

Batas Komonwelt Blg. 184 o Commonwealth Act No. 184

Ito ang nagtatag sa ahensyang Surian ng Wikang Pambansa at nagbigay rito ng kapangyarihan na magsagawa ng pag-aaral sa mga wika sa bansa.
