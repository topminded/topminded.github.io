16. procedure of oyster cocktail​


Ingredients

24oz fresh oysters, no shells

1large red onion, chopped

1green pepper, chopped

1yellow pepper, chopped

3tablespoons lemon juicw

1teaspoon rocoto paste (hot Peruvian chili)

2tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

1/2cup tomato sauce

1/2cup cocktail sauce

1tablespoon sweet chili sauce

1/4cup chopped fresh cilantro

Salt and pepper to taste

Coarse salt (if desired, for garnish)

lemon slices (if desired, for garnish)

Directions

1. In large bowl, mix all ingredients. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

2. Preferably serve in Martini glasses. Rub the rim of the glasses with the lemon juice and coarse salt.

3. Garnish with chopped cilantro and a lemon wedge on rim of the glass.
