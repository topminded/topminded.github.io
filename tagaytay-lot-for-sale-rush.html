Answer:

D. 108

Step-by-step explanation:

L - 15m

W - 12m

[tex]15 \times 2 = 30 [/tex]

[tex]12 \times 2 = 24[/tex]

Since the lot is in rectangular, each sides are not in equal length and width, so we have a 2 in each sides.

[tex]30 + 24 = 54[/tex]

54m is the sum of the product of L and W

[tex]54 \times 2 = 108[/tex]

Since the problem stated that Peter wishes to put 2 layer barb wire fence, multiply the sum of total meters of the rectangular lot.
