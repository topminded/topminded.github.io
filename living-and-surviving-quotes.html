24. Quotes: THE PURPOSE OF OUR LIVES IS TO BE HAPPY :) - DALAI LAMA -


Answer:

Many people do not think about the purpose of life or if they do, they find purpose in family or work. Often the blind acquisition of material goods or earning money may be a purpose. Happiness is considered by many to be a lucky condition enjoyed occasionally by chance circumstances.

However the Dalai Lama here reminds us that the whole purpose of life is to be happy. A deceptively simple yet deep assertion. To be continually happy is not easy for most people. Worries about money, family or work can upset our mental and emotional balance.

So we need activities which increase our feelings of self-worth and positivity. For many people being in nature and physical exercise bring about these feelings. They lower mental tension, raise energy levels and increase endorphin levels in the brain. The result is an increase in happiness.

-qtie3066

Explanation:

This quote is by Dalai Lama XIV from one of his many writings.

The big idea here is understand that we really need to focus on being happy from an internal loci of control.

Here’s how this applies to us and self-improvement:

They say happiness is a pursuit and not a destination. That is correct.

They say we should strive for joy on a day to day basis and not suffer on a daily basis with the expectation that one day you will be happy. That is also correct.

But attaining that state of joy or happiness or whatever you want to call it, is your responsibility. You have to be accountable for it. You cannot let happiness come to you. You have to live your life in a way that brings you joy.

So, let’s put this into action:

Stop complaining that the circumstances responsible for your happiness are not under your control. Don’t fall for the victim mentality.

Once you understand that however bad your current situation is you can start to take control, i.e. come up with a plan to become happy.

You will fail, at least initially, but you will get up, dust the failure off, and try again.
