23. What is known as the building blocks of life?CellOrganOrgan systemsTissue​


Cell is known as the building blocks of life.

About cell of body

The cell is the smallest structural and functional unit as a constituent of living organisms at the organizational level in life. The word cell itself is taken from the Greek, namely 'Cellula' attua 'Cella' which means 'empty space / small rooms'.

All the functions in life take place in cells, not only that, cells also play a role in regulating the life of an organism in this world.

All living organisms are composed of cells, because cells have an important role for every living thing in its survival.

Organisms have a body, the body of the organism itself consists of organ systems, then the organ systems are composed by organs, then organs are formed by a network, and the existence of tissues comes from cells. So that we can draw the conclusion that the cell is the main constituent of the body of living things.

Cells have a very important task, which is to organize and process all the information in the body of a living thing so that every living thing is able to carry out its life functions properly.

Classification of Cells Based on the Number

1. Unicellular

Unicellular is a living thing that only has 1 single cell.

2. Multicellular

Multicellular is a living thing that has many cells that make up its body.
