1. List down the roles and responsibilities of each housekeeping personnel???1. Executive Housekeeper 2. Assistant Housekeeper 3. Floor Supervisor 4. Guestroom Attendants 5. Public Area Supervisor ​


Explanation:

1. Executive housekeepers direct and control housekeeping operations and staff of the housekeeping department. An executive housekeeper, coordinates between housekeeping crews to inspect assigned areas to ensure standards are met. An executive housekeeper manages many priorities and demands and is able to solve problems, support staff, as well as perform the duties of a housekeeper when required.

2. Assistant Housekeeper is responsible for:

Performance of regular and irregular inspections of your workplace.

Supervision of Housekeeping staff.

The development of housekeeping training sessions.

The upkeep of all training forms used.

Training the new hires according to the Housekeeping Manual.

3. Floor Supervisor Duties and Responsibilities                                            Inspect Guest Areas for Cleanliness. ...

Greet and Build Rapport With Guests. ...

Monitor Establishment's Financial Performance. ...

Perform Opening and Closing Procedures. ...

Diffuse Customer Complaints. ...

Delegate Basic Tasks to Staff

4. Room Attendant

Stock and sort supplies.

Vacuum, clean, dust and polish guest rooms.

Make beds, change sheets, remove and replace used towels and toiletries.

Deliver and retrieve items on loan to guests e.g. iron and ironing boards.

Ensure security of guest rooms and privacy of guest

5. The main duty of a public area supervisor is to maintain order, but some other minor duties are to supervise cleanliness of the area and maintenance. Required to have good knowledge of all hotel facilities, hours of operation, Restaurants, shops and function rooms.

19. Why is it necessary for the housekeeping supervisor to ensure employees discipline in the workplace? ​


Answer:

Why is it necessary for the housekeeping supervisor to ensure employees' discipline in the workplace? ​

Individuals that are disciplined at work conduct in a professional manner and follow the organization's norms and regulations. It is necessary for the housekeeping supervisor to ensure the employees' discipline in the workplace because discipline and timeliness are two of the most important characteristics for a successful professional. Misconduct at work tarnishes the entire workplace atmosphere, resulting in zero or very low performance. Discipline is important in the workplace because it ensures that employees act properly and maintain the workplace's decency. Discipline is crucial because it brings employees together and inspires them to respect their employers.
