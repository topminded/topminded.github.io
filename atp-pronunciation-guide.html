D.38 ATP

Explanation:

Some of the energy from the electrons is used to pump hydrogen ions across the membrane, creating an electrochemical gradient that drives the synthesis of many more molecules of ATP. In all three stages of cellular respiration combined, as many as 36 molecules of ATP are produced from just one molecule of glucose.
