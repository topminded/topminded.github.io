1. What does "du m spiro, spero." mean?


What does "đum spiro, spero." mean?

Đum spiro, spero means "While I breathe, I hope". It is a Latin phrase used as a motto. It means that as long as an individual lives, he/she will keep hoping and striving for success. The motto "đum spiro, spero" means not giving up as long as we're living.

Answer:

4. Which of the following describes the compound below? bridged bicyclic bridged tricyclic spiro bicyclic fused bicyclic


Answer:

Bridged Bicyclic

A bicyclic molecule in which a one or more atoms spans (bridges) another ring of atoms. Bicyclo heptane is a bridged bicyclic cycloalkane.

In bridged bicyclic compounds, the two rings share three or more atoms, separating the two bridgehead atoms by a bridge containing at least one atom.
