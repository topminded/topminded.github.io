5. 2. The density of coconut oil is 0.92 g/mL. What volume will 80.0 g of coconut oil occupy? ​


Answer:

The volume is 86.96 mL.

Explanation:

Given:

ρ = 0.92 g/mL

m = 80.0 g

Required:

V = ?

Solution:

ρ = m/V

V = m/ρ

V = 80.0 g / 0.92 g/mL

V = 86.96 mL
