19. what is a vegetation steak​


What is a vegetation steak​?

Vegetation or vegetarian steaks are steaks that processed to capture all the qualities of real beef but without the guilt of actually eating it, a very important stand taken by vegans who detest the consumption of animal products. These steaks are made by combining different plant-based food like chickpeas, cauliflower, yeast, mushrooms, garlic, and the usual spices. The point is to produce something that looks, taste, smell and feel like a steak.
