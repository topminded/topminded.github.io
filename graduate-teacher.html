5. A group of 17 teachers and students together is preparing the stage for graduation ceremonies. The number of teacher is 19 less than twice the number of students. How many students are there? How many teachers are there?


Answer:

5 teachers. 12 students.

Step-by-step explanation:

Equation:

(total number of people) = (number of teachers) + (number of students)

17 = (2n-19) + n where n is number of students

17 = 3n-19

36 = 3n

12 = n (so 12 students)

Now check

17-12=2(12)-19

5=24-19

5=5

5 is the number of teachers.
