30. subtract 1/3 from 6 1/2. A) 6 1/3 B) 6 1/6 C) 5 1/6​


Answer:

B.)  6  1/6

Step-by-step explanation:

First, set aside the whole number which is 6

Then, subtract the fractions. But, you can't easily subtract fraction when it is a dissimilar fraction. So change it into similar fractions.  1/2  &  1/3  =  3/6  &  2/6

Now, subtract the fractions.     3/6  -  2/6 = 1/6

Lastly, add the whole number from the fraction.
