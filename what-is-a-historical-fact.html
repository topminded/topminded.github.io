Historical facts are preserved in the present day through various means such as museums, archives, libraries, and historical landmarks. These institutions and structures serve as reminders of past events, allowing people to appreciate and learn from the lessons of history. Additionally, historians and researchers use primary sources like letters, journals, photographs, and other records to reconstruct historical events and understand the past accurately.Historical documents play a crucial role in Philippine society as they help to connect Filipinos with their cultural and national identity. These documents serve as evidence of the country's rich history, traditions, and beliefs, providing insights into the lives of Filipinos in the past. They also help to shape the collective memory of the Filipino people, creating a sense of shared history and heritage. Moreover, historical documents are valuable tools for education and research, enabling scholars to learn about the past and inform decision-making in the present. By preserving and studying historical documents, the Philippine society can continue to honor its past and build a better future.
