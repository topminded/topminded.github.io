5. __________is the study of how to mannage oneself ethically, professionally and in a clinically sound manner via online and digital mediums. a.Digital Native b. Digital Devices c. Internet d. Digital Ethics​


Answer:

The correct answer is d. Digital Ethics.

Digital ethics is the study of how to manage oneself ethically, professionally, and in a clinically sound manner through online and digital mediums. It involves considering the ethical implications of using technology and the internet in various contexts, such as communication, education, healthcare, and business. Digital ethics also involves being mindful of the impact of technology on society and the potential consequences of our actions online.

Digital ethics is important because technology has become an integral part of many aspects of our lives, and it is essential to consider the ethical implications of how we use it. Digital ethics helps individuals and organizations to make informed decisions about how to use technology responsibly and ethically, and to consider the potential consequences of their actions.

17. Ano poh ibig sbhn ng digital ethical


Answer:

"Digital ethics" refers to the ethical principles and values that guide the behavior of individuals and organizations in the digital realm. It encompasses a range of issues related to the use of technology, including privacy, security, responsibility, transparency, and accountability.

Digital ethics involves considering the potential impact of our actions in the digital world on other people, society, and the environment. It requires individuals and organizations to think critically about how they use technology and to make decisions that reflect their ethical values.

Examples of digital ethical issues include:

1.Protecting personal data and privacy online.

2.Ensuring the security of digital systems and networks

3.Avoiding online harassment, bullying, and hate speech

4.Promoting online civility and respect

5.Providing equal access to digital resources and technology

6.Encouraging responsible use of digital resources and technology

7.Ensuring transparency and accountability in the use of digital technology

Overall, digital ethics is an important consideration in today's increasingly digital world, and requires individuals and organizations to act responsibly and ethically in their use of technology.
